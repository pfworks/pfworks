                 Rib Steak with Orange Bell Pepper Puree

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4                     rib steaks
  1           teaspoon  kosher salt
                        olive oil
                        peanut oil (if frying)
                        freshly ground black pepper
                        Puree
  1                     orange bell pepper -- halved and seeded
  5        tablespoons  olive oil
  1         tablespoon  habanero sauce
  1           teaspoon  kosher salt

Prepare steaks by salting them, then let them come to room temperature. Rub with olive oil before grilling.

Coat bell pepper with olive oil, and then blacken it. Chop it up, and stick it in food processor with remaining oil, hot sauce and salt. Blend until smooth.

Grill steaks and serve with puree on top.
