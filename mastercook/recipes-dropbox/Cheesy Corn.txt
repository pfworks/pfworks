                               Cheesy Corn

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  butter
  4          teaspoons  flour
     1/8      teaspoon  garlic powder
     3/4           cup  milk
  1 1/2           cups  sharp cheddar cheese
  3             ounces  cream cheese -- cubed and room temp
  30            ounces  frozen white shoepeg corn kernels -- thawed and drained
  3             ounces  country ham or bacon -- diced
  1         tablespoon  hot pepper sauce

Preheat oven to 350.

In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Once melted, stir in the flour and garlic powder. While constantly stirring, add the milk. When the mixture is thick, stir in the cream and cheddar cheeses stirring constantly to avoid burning. Add the corn, ham, and hot sauce; fold until completely mixed.

Pour mixture into baking dish, smooth with back of spatula, and bake for 45 minutes.
