                          Southern Creamed Corn

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8                ear  corn -- husked
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  1         tablespoon  all-purpose flour
                        salt and pepper
  1                cup  heavy cream
     1/2           cup  cold water
  2        tablespoons  bacon grease
  1         tablespoon  butter

In a large bowl, cut the tip off the cob. Cut kernels. Scrape blade against cob to press out milky liquid.

Whisk together sugar, flour, salt, and pepper. Combine with corn. Add heavy cream and water. Mix.

In a large skillet over medium heat, heat bacon grase. Add corn mixture and turn heat to med-low, stirring until it becomes creamy, about 30 minutes.

Add butter right before serving.
