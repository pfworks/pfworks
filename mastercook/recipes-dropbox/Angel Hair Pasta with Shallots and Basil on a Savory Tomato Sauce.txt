    Angel Hair Pasta with Shallots and Basil on a Savory Tomato Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  2        tablespoons  fresh garlic -- finely chopped
  2        tablespoons  shallot -- finely chopped
  2        tablespoons  fresh basil -- julienned
     1/4           cup  tomatoes -- diced, skins and seeds removed
  1              pound  angel hair pasta -- al dente
                        cracked black pepper
     1/2           cup  Savory Tomato Sauce
  4             sprigs  basil
     1/2           cup  parmesan cheese -- freshly grated

In a large skillet place the olive oil and heat it on medium high heat until hot. Add the garlic, shallots, julienned basil and tomatoes. Saute for 2-3 minutes, or until tomatoes are soft but still hold their shape.

Add the angel hair pasta and cracked black pepper, and toss them in.

In the bottom of each of 4 soup bowls, place 2 tablespoons of the Savory Tomato Sauce. Place the pasta on top and garnish with a basil sprig.

Serve with parmesan cheese and cracked black pepper on the side.

                           Savory Tomato Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
     1/4           cup  onion -- coarsely chopped
  2        tablespoons  garlic -- coarsely chopped
  2        tablespoons  shallot -- coarsely chopped
  2        tablespoons  fresh basil -- coarsely chopped
  2          teaspoons  fresh rosemary
     1/2           cup  fresh tomatoes (in juice) -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  V-8® vegetable juice
  1           teaspoon  sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  nutmeg
     1/4           cup  white wine
                        salt
                        black pepper

In a medium saucepan place the olive oil and heat it on medium high until hot. Add the onions, garlic and shallots. Saute for 3-4 minutes, or until onions are translucent.

Add basil and rosemary, and saute for 1 minute.

Add the remainder. Cook slowly on medium low heat for 15 minutes.

Pour sauce into food processor and blend. Strain thru fine sieve.

Yield:
  "2 cups"
