            Grilled Rib-Eye Steaks wirth Parsley-Garlic Butter

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  butter -- softened
  1         tablespoon  fresh parsley -- finely chopped
  1         tablespoon  fresh chives -- chopped
  1              clove  garlic -- pressed
  2          teaspoons  cognac
                        salt and pepper
  3             pounds  rib-eye

Mix together butter mixture and chill.

Salt and pepper steaks and grill over med-high heat.

Top steak with spoonful of butter and serve.
