                                Maid-Rites

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  ground beef
  2        tablespoons  vinegar (not white)
  1         tablespoon  instant minced onion
  1           teaspoon  salt
  1 1/2           cups  chicken broth -- boiling
  2        tablespoons  ketchup
  2        tablespoons  mustard

Brown meat lightly; drain fat. Add vinegar, onion, salt, ketchup and mustard. Stir well. Add boiling broth. Cover and simmer until meat is tender and liquid is absorbed. Serve on hamburger buns with chopped onions, pickles, ketchup and mustard.
