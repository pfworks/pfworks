                        Black Beans in Mango Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/2    tablespoons  olive oil
  2               cups  minced red onion
  1                     jalapeno -- minced
  1 1/2    tablespoons  ginger -- minced
  1 1/2      teaspoons  cumin seed
  1 1/2      teaspoons  salt
  3         15 oz cans  cooked black beans -- rinsed and drained
  6        tablespoons  lime juice
  2                     ripe mangoes -- minced
                        black pepper
                        cilantro
                        lime wedge

heat oil in med skillet. add 1.75 cups of onion, garlic, chile, ginger, cumin seeds and salt. Sautee over med heat for 3 minutes.

heat down to med-low, add black beans and half lime juice. saute for 5 minutes, mash beans slightly and put in bowl.

stir in remaining lime juice and about half mangos into beans, mashing a bit. Grind in pepper and cover and let stand for 15 minutes.

Top with l/o red onion and mango.
