                         Crock Pot Pizza Burgers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  ground beef -- seasoned to taste
  22 1/2        ounces  pizza sauce (6n1 tomatoes)
  1                cup  mozzarella cheese -- shredded
  3             ounces  pepperoni -- diced
  1           teaspoon  italian seasoning
  6                     buns
  6             slices  mozzarella cheese

Brown beef, seasoning as desired.

Add drained ground beef to your slow cooker and combine with remaining infredients except for buns and cheese slices, adding whatever other pizza toppings you want.

Cook on low for 4 hours.

Serve on buns topped with cheese slices.
