                              Bakers Waffles

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/2           cups  flour
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  1         tablespoon  baking powder
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
  1 1/4           cups  buttermilk
     1/2           cup  vegetable oil
  2              large  egg -- separated

For the waffles. stir the flour, baking powder and salt together. In a separate bowl, whisk the buttermilk, oil and egg yolks together. Add this to the dry mixture and stir gently until blended.

In another bowl, whisk egg whites until a soft peak forms. Fold into batter.

Preheat and grease iron. Make waffles.
