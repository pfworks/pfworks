                 Grilled Lemon Oregano Chicken Drumsticks

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  fresh lemon juice
  2        tablespoons  fresh oregano -- coarsely chopped
  2             cloves  garlic -- pressed
  1         tablespoon  extra virgin olive oil
  1 1/2      teaspoons  kosher salt
  1           teaspoon  lemon peel -- freshly grated
  8                     chicken drumsticks

Whisk first 6 ingredients in small bowl. Season to taste with freshly ground black pepper. Pour marinade into 1 gallon resealable plastic bag; add chicken and seal. Turn bag to coat chicken. Marinate at room temp 30 minutes or in fridge for up to 2 days, turning occasionally.

Grill chicken, turning frequently till cooked through and golden, about 30 minutes.
