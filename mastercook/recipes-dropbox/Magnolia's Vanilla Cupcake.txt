                        Magnolia's Vanilla Cupcake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        cupcakes
  1 1/2           cups  self-rising flour
  1 1/4           cups  all-purpose flour
  1                cup  unsalted butter -- softened
  2               cups  sugar
  4              large  egg -- room temp
  1                cup  milk
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
                        icing
  1                cup  unsalted butter
  8               cups  powdered sugar
     1/2           cup  milk
  2          teaspoons  vanilla extract

Preheat oven to 350.

In a small bowl, combine the flours.

In an electric mixer on med speed, cream butter. Add sugar and beat until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time. Add the dry ingredients in 3 parts, alternating with milk and vanilla. Do not over beat. Scrape sides. Spoon batter into cupcake liners about 3/4 full. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Cool in tins for 15 minutes; cool completely on rack before icing.

Place the butter in a large mixing bowl. Add 4 cups of sugar and then the milk and vanilla. On med speed of mixer, beat until smooth and creamy, about 3-5 minutes. Gradually add remaining sugar, 1 cup at a time until thick enough.

Yield:
  "24 "
