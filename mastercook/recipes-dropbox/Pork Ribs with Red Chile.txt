                         Pork Ribs with Red Chile

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  Salsa Adobo
  1 1/2           cups  vinegar
  2          teaspoons  salt
  2          teaspoons  dried mexican oregano
  3             pounds  pork ribs

Combine salsa, vinegar, salt and oregano to make marinade. Rub marinade into ribs and refirigerate at least 4 hours, turning occassionally. Roast at 350 for 1 hour or until tender.
