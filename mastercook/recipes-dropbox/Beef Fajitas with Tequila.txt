                        Beef Fajitas with Tequila

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             pounds  skirt steak
  3             cloves  garlic -- peeled and mashed
     1/3           cup  vegetable oil
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil
  4        tablespoons  fresh lime juice
  4        tablespoons  tequila
  1           teaspoon  ground cumin
  1           teaspoon  salt
  3                     bell pepper -- cut into strips
  1              large  white onion -- sliced into crescents

Cut the steak into pieces that will fit on your grill or grill pan.

Place pieces of meat in a non-reactive bowl.

Mix the garlic, 1/3 cup vegetgable oil, lime juice, tequila, cumin and salt.

Pour over meat and marinade for 2 hours.

Remove meat from marinade and grill over med-high heat.

Heat remaing 2 tbs of oil and saute peppers and onions over med-high heat until crisp-tender.

Cut the meat into strips and serve with veges.
