                             Creole Croutons

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  3/4" cubed dried French or Italian white bread, crust removed
  1 1/2    tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
     1/2      teaspoon  angel dust cajun seasoning

Heat oil in large saute pan over med-high heat for 1 minute. Add bread cubes, stirring and tossing in pan to evenly coat the bread with oil. SPrinkle in the Cajun seasoning while stirring continuously. Toast for 5 minutes, until golden brown.
