                Grilled Giant Pork Chops with Adobo Paste

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2    tablespoons  olive oil
  2        tablespoons  paprika
  1 1/2    tablespoons  dried oregano -- crumbled
  1         tablespoon  garlic -- minced
  1 1/2      teaspoons  ground cumin
  1 1/2      teaspoons  dried crushed red pepper
  1 1/2      teaspoons  fresh lime zest -- finely grated
  1 1/2      teaspoons  kosher salt
     1/4      teaspoon  black pepper
  4                     bone-in loin pork chops -- 3/4 lb each 1-1.5" thick

Prepare grill

Stir together all ingredients except chops in a bowl to form paste, then rub paste all over chops.

Oil grill rack, then grill chops over coals, turning occasionally, until browned, 2 to 3 minutes total.

Move chops to area with no coals underneath, then cover with inverted roasting pan and grill, turning chops once, until 145, 8 to 10 minutes. Transfer to platter and let stand 5 minutes.
