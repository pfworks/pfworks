                           Spicy Molasses Glaze

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  molasses
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  2                     serrano pepper -- minced
  1                 in  fresh ginger -- peeled and grated
     1/2      teaspoon  crushed red pepper
     3/4           cup  extra virgin olive oil
     1/2    tablespoon  coarse salt
                        black pepper

Whisk molasses, garlic, chiles, ginger, pepper flakes, and oil in a small bowl Season with salt and pepper. Keep sealed at room temp for up to a week.

Yield:
  "1 cup"
