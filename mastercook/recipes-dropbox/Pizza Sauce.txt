                               Pizza Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8             ounces  can tomato sauce
  7 1/2         ounces  can undrained tomatoes, cut up
     1/2           cup  chopped onion
  1         tablespoon  crushed dried basil
  1           teaspoon  sugar
  1           teaspoon  crushed dried oregano
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
     1/4      teaspoon  black pepper

Combine all ingredients in medium saucepan. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until onion is tender.
