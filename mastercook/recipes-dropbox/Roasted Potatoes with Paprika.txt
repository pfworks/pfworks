                      Roasted Potatoes with Paprika

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3                     potatoes -- peeled and sliced into thin rounds
  4             cloves  garlic -- chopped
  3        tablespoons  chopped fresh parsley
  3        tablespoons  olive oil
     1/2      teaspoon  smoked spanish paprika

Preheat oven to 350.. In a bowl, combine potatoes, garlic, parsley and olive oil. Mix well. Place in a single layer on a nonstick baking pan and sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 15-20 minutes until crunchy.
