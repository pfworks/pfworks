                          Classic Cheese Crisps

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4             medium  flour tortilla -- thin
  4          teaspoons  unsalted butter -- melted
  2               cups  grated cheese

Preheat oven to 325.

Brush tortillas on both sides with butter. Place on a wire rack on a baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway thru.

Remove from oven and spread with cheese and chiles, if desired.

Turn up oven to 500.

Cook for about 5 minutes, until cheese bubbles.
