                              Best Ever Cake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 24    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  sugar
  2               cups  flour
  2          teaspoons  baking soda
  2                     eggs
  1                can  (16 or 20 oz) crushed pineapple, undrained
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
			
                        icing
  4             ounces  butter
  8             ounces  cream cheese -- room temp
  1 1/2           cups  powdered sugar
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
  1                cup  walnuts -- chopped

Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour a 9x13 baking dish.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the sugar, flour, baking soda, eggs, pineapple and vanilla. Mix well and pour into the prepared baking dish. Bake for 40-45 minutes. Let cool 10-15 minutes before icing.

Combine all of the icing ingredients, except the nuts, and beat well. Spread evenly over cake and top with nuts.
