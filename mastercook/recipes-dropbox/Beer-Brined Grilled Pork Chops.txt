                      Beer-Brined Grilled Pork Chops

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  water
  2               cups  dark lager beer
     1/4           cup  coarse salt
  3        tablespoons  dark brown sugar -- packed
  3        tablespoons  molasses
  1                cup  ice cube
  6                     1.25" thick center cut bone-in pork chops
  7             cloves  garlic -- minced
  3          teaspoons  coarsely ground black pepper
  2          teaspoons  salt
  2          teaspoons  dried sage leaves

Combine 2 cups water, beer, 1/4 cup coarse salt, sugar and molasses in large bowl. Stir until salt and sugar dissolve. Stir in ice. Place pork chops in large resealable plastic bag. Pour beer brine over chops; seal bag. Refrigerate 4 hours, turning occasionally.

Prepare BBQ for med-high heat. Remove pork chops from brine, pat dry. Mix garlic, pepper, salt and sage in small bowl. Rub mixture over both sides of pork chops. Grill until insides are 145, about 10 minutes per side. Transfer to platter, cover with foil and let rest 5 minutes.
