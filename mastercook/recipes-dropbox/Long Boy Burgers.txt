                             Long Boy Burgers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2                     6 inch sub rolls, split in half
  1 1/4           cups  cornflakes -- crushed
     1/2         small  onion -- minced
     1/3           cup  milk
  6        tablespoons  ketchup
  1         tablespoon  worcestershire sauce
  1           teaspoon  fresh thyme -- minced
     1/2      teaspoon  garlic powder
  1              large  egg -- lightly beaten
                        salt and pepper
  1 1/2         pounds  90 percent lean ground beef
  2               cups  cheddar cheese -- shredded

Adjust oven rack to upper-middle position and heat oven to 450. Bake rolls, cut side up, on wire rack set inside rimmed baking sheet until golden and crisp, about 5 minutes.

Combine cornflakes, onion, milk, 4 tbspns ketchup, worcestshire sauce, thyme, garlic powder, egg, 1/2 tspn salt, and 1 tspn pepper in large bowl. Add meat and knead gently until well-combined.

Top toasted rolls evenly with meat mixture, spreading meat to edges of rolls. Brush top of meat evenly with remaining ketchup and bake until meat registers 160, 20 to 25 minutes. Top with cheese and continue to bake until cheese melts, 3 to 4 minutes. Let rest 5 minutes. Serve.
