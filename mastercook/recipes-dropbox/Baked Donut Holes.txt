                            Baked Donut Holes

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  shortening
  1                cup  sugar
  2              large  eggs
  3               cups  all-purpose flour
  4          teaspoons  baking powder
  1           teaspoon  salt
  1           teaspoon  ground nutmeg
  1                cup  milk
     1/2           cup  butter -- melted
  6        tablespoons  sugar
  1         tablespoon  ground cinnamon

Beat shortening and 1 cup sugar at medium speed with an electric mixer until creamy. Add eggs and beat well.

Combine flour and next 3 ingredients; add to sugar mixture alternatively with milk, beginning and ending with flour mixture. Spoon batter by level tablespoons into lightly greased mini (1.75") muffin pans.

Bake at 425 for 10 minutes. Remove from pans, and place on wire rack. Brush tops with melted butter.

Combine 6 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Roll tops of warm donut hols in mixture. Serve warm.

Yield:
  "3 1/2 dozen"
