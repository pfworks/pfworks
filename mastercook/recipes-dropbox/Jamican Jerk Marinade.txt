                          Jamican Jerk Marinade

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                     sweet onion -- chopped
  4                     green onion -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  fresh thyme or 2 tspns dried
  4          teaspoons  ground allspice
  1           teaspoon  salt
  1           teaspoon  ground nutmeg
  1           teaspoon  ground cinnamon
  1           teaspoon  ground black pepper
  6                     habanero chile -- seeded
  4                     garlic clove -- peeled
  2        tablespoons  fresh ginger -- grated
  2        tablespoons  lime juice
     1/2           cup  red wine vinegar
     1/4           cup  olive oil
     1/4           cup  soy sauce
     1/4           cup  dark rum
  2        tablespoons  packed brown sugar

In food processor, combine sweet onion, green onions, thyme, allspice, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon, pepper, chiles, garlic, ginger, lime juice, vinegar, oil, soy sauce, rum and brown sugar; process until smooth.
