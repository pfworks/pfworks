                     Lunch Counter Egg Salad Sandwich

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4              large  egg -- hard boiled, peeled and coarsely chopped
  1                cup  celery -- finely chopped
  1         tablespoon  relish
     1/2           cup  mayonnaise
  8             slices  good sandwich bread -- lightly toasted
  4                     lettuce leaf
                        salt and pepper

In a medium bowl, mix eggs, celery, relish and mayonnaise.

Divind evenly among sandwiches and top each with lettuce, salt and pepper.
