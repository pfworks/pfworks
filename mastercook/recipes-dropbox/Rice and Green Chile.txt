                           Rice and Green Chile

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  sour cream
  2               cups  raw instant rice
  4             ounces  diced green chiles (2 cans)
  1              pound  Monterey jack cheese -- grated or shredded

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a bowl, mix all ingredients together, then pour into a greased casserole dish. Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes, until bubbly.
