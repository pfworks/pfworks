                               Eclair Lake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2                     3.4 oz packages Vanilla Instant Pudding Mix
  3 1/2           cups  whole milk
  12            ounces  cool whip
  2                     14.4 oz packages graham crackers
                        frosting
  3        tablespoons  butter
  3        tablespoons  milk
  3        tablespoons  cocoa
     1/2           cup  powdered sugar

Blend milk and pudding packets for about 2 minutes. Fold in Cool Whip.

In a 9x13 pan, layer crackers to cover entire bottom.

Pour half of pudding mixture on top and level.

Put another layer of crackers, then pour second half of pudding on top and level.

Put final layer of crackers on top, bumpy side down.

Microwave butter and milk until just melted. Add cocoa and powdered sugar and blend. Pour over top of cake and smooth out. Refrigerate overnight.
