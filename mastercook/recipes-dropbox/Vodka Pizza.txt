                               Vodka Pizza

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  4             cloves  garlic -- minced
  1           teaspoon  dried oregano
     1/2      teaspoon  red pepper flakes
  28            ounces  whole peeled tomatoes -- broken
  1                cup  heavy cream
     1/3           cup  vodka
                        kosher salt and pepper
  1                     new york pizza dough
  1              pound  fresh mozzarella
  16                    basil leaf
                        kosher salt

Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat until shimmering. Add garlic, oregano, and pepper flakes and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes. Increase heat to high, bring to a boil, then reduce to a bare simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, until reduced bu 1/4, about 20 minutes. Add cream, increase heat to bring a boil, reduce to a simmer, and continue to cook until reduced again by 1/4, about 20 minutes longer. Add vodka and cook for 7 minutes. Remove from heat and season to taste with salt and pepper.

Let sauce cool slightly, then transfer half to blender. Blend, on lowest speed and increase to high until completely smooth. Strain into a medium bowl. Repeat with other half of sauce.

Preheat oven with stone to 550 for 45 minutes.

Prepare dough for pizza.

Spread about 3/4 cup sauce evenly over crust, with a 1/2" border. Place 8-10 chunks of mozzarella evenly over surface and scatter with 4-5 basil leaves. Drizzle 1 tbspn oil and sprinkle with salt. Cook for 6-12 minutes.
