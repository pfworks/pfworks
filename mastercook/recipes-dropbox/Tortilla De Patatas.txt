                           Tortilla De Patatas

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3             pounds  Yukon Gold potato (6-8) -- peeled and cut into 1/4" slices
  2          teaspoons  kosher salt
  2               cups  olive
  1             medium  onion -- chopped
  10                    eggs -- beated to blend

Place potatoes in a large colander, sprinkle with 2 tspns salt, and toss to coat. Let stand for 30 minutes. Pat dry.

Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over med0high heat. Add potatoes and cook, turning occasionally until crisp-tender, 10-15 minutes.

Add onions; cook until onion and potatoes are soft, 6-8 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to a large bowl; cool slightly. Strain oil into heatproof bowl; set aside.

Add eggs to potato mixture; season with salt. Wipe skillet. Heat 1/4 cup of reserved oil over med-high heat. Add egg mixture, arranging potatoes to submerge.

Reduce heat to medium; cook tortilla until almost set, 25-30 minutes. Invert onto large plate. Slide into skillet brown side up. Cook until golden on bottom and cooked through, about 4 minutes more. Serve warm or room temp. Cut into wedges.
