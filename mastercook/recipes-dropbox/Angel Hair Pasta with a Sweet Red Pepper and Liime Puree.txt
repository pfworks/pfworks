         Angel Hair Pasta with a Sweet Red Pepper and Liime Puree

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  6              large  red bell pepper -- roasted, peeled and seeded
  1           teaspoon  fresh garlic -- chopped
     1/4           cup  cilantro -- chopped
     1/4           cup  lime juice
     1/2      teaspoon  cayenne pepper
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  1              pound  angel hair pasta -- al dente and drained
     1/2           cup  parmesan cheese

In a food processor, place the roasted red bell peppers, garlic, cilantro, lime juice, cayenne pepper, and salt. Blend for 60 seconds, or until roughly pureed.

In a medium large saute pan place the olive oil and heat it on medium high until hot. Add the puree mixture and saute it for 5 minutes, or until hot.

Add angel hair pasta and quickly mix with puree.

Add parmesan cheese and mix it in.
