                             Chocolate Oaties

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  sugar
     1/4           cup  cocoa
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
     1/2           cup  milk
     1/4         pound  butter
  1           teaspoon  vanilla
     1/2           cup  peanut butter
  3               cups  oatmeal

Mix first 5 ingredients together in saucepan and bring to a boil. Cook 1 minute, stirring.

Remove from heat, and add the rest of the ingredients and mix together.

Drop spoonfuls onto wax paper and let cool.
