                     Chicken with Tomatoes and Honey

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  sunflower oil
  2        tablespoons  butter
  4                     chicken quarters
  1                     onion -- finely chopped
  1              clove  garlic -- crushed
  1           teaspoon  cinnamon -- ground
  1              pinch  ginger -- ground
  3             pounds  tomatoes -- peeled, cored and roughly chopped
  2        tablespoons  honey
  1         tablespoon  sesame seed
                        salt and pepper
                        flat leaf parsley -- chopped

Place the oil and butter in a large, flameproof casserole. Add the chicken and cook over a medium heat for about 3 minutes until it is lightly browned.

Add the onion, garlic, cinnamon, tomatoes and seasoning, and heat gently until the tomatoes begin to bubble.

Lower the heat and then cover. Simmer very gently for about 1 hour, stirring and turning the chicken occasionally, or until it is completely cooked through.

Lift out the chicken pieces and transfer to a plate. Increat the heat and cook the tomato sauce until is is reduced to a thick puree, stirring often. Stir in the honey, cook for 1 minute, then return the chicken pieced to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes.

Transfer to platter and sprinkle with sesame seeds and parsley.
