                Grilled Rib-Eye Brochettes with Charmoula

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 15    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4             ounces  flat leaf parsley -- stemmed and coarsely chopped
  4             ounces  cilantro -- coarsely chopped
  3             cloves  garlic -- minced
  2        tablespoons  ground cumin
  1         tablespoon  ground coriander
  2        tablespoons  sweet paprika
  1           teaspoon  smoked paprika
  1           teaspoon  cayenne
  1              pinch  saffron threads
  1                cup  olive oil
     1/4           cup  fresh lemon juice
                        kosher salt
  6                     1.5" thick boneless rib-eye steaks (7.5 lbs) -- trimmed and cut into 1.5 or 2 inch pieces.
  2              small  red onion -- 1.5" pieces
  2              large  red bell pepper -- 1.5" pieces

In a food processor, combine the parsley with the cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprikas, cayenne and saffron and pulse until finely chopped. Add lemon juice and 1 cup of the oil and pulse to incorporate. Add 1 tbspn of salt. Scrape half of this charmoula into a bowl and refrigerate.

Scrape remaining charmoula into a very large bowl. Add steak cubes and turn to coat. Let sit at room temperature for 2 hours or refrigerate for 4 hours.

Put three chunks of meat onto a skewer with an onion piece and pepper piece between each one. Grill over high heat for about 15 minutes. Serve with reserved sauce.
