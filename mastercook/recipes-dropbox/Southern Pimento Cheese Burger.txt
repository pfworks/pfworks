                      Southern Pimento Cheese Burger

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  6             ounces  sharp cheddar cheese -- shredded
  4             ounces  monterey jack cheese -- shredded
  3        tablespoons  mayonnaise
  3        tablespoons  cream cheese -- softened
  4             ounces  diced pimentos -- drained well
     1/8      teaspoon  cayenne
  1 1/4         pounds  ground chuck
  1           teaspoon  worcestershire sauce
  1           teaspoon  kosher salt
     1/2      teaspoon  black pepper
                        red leaf lettuce
  4                     burger bun

Combine cheeses, mayonnaise, cream cheese, pimentos and cayenne pepper in a medium bow and mix together using a wooden spoon.

Work worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper into ground chuck. Shape into 4 patties. Make a depression in the center of each patty. 

Grill burgers for 4-6 minutes per side. With a minute or two left, top each burger with 1/4 cup pimento cheese.

To serve, place a leaf of lettuce on the bottom of a bun and top with burger.
