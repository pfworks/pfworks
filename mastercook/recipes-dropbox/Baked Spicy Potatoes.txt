                           Baked Spicy Potatoes

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4        tablespoons  dijon mustard
  1           teaspoon  cumin
     1/2      teaspoon  cayenne pepper
  2          teaspoons  paprika
  1           teaspoon  chile powder
  16             small  red potatoes

Preheat oven to 400. Spray baking pan with vegetable oil. Place mustard and spices in bowl to blend. Make deep holes in potatoes with tines of fork. Add potoatoes and toss to coat. Pour into baking pan and spread potatoes. Bake about 1 hour.
