                       Pearl Onions in Cream Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  10            ounces  Pearl Onions
  3        tablespoons  butter
  3        tablespoons  flour
  1 1/2           cups  milk -- heated
  2        tablespoons  cream sherry
  1               drop  tabasco sauce
  1               dash  ground nutmeg
                        white pepper
                        salt
                        parsley -- chopped

Drop peeled onions in boiling water. Cook for 10 minutes.

Melt butter over med-high heat. Add flour until all butter is absorbed, stirring with wire whisk.

Turn heat down to low, add milk slowly while stirring.

Add sherry, tabasco sauce nutmeg pepper and salt. Fold in cooked onions and cook well.
