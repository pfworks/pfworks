                        Baked Cauliflower Poppers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2              heads  cauliflower
     1/4           cup  olive oil
  1 1/2      teaspoons  salt
  1           teaspoon  pepper

Preheat oven to 400.

Wash and clean cauliflower. Cut into small pieces.

In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, salt and pepper, Add caulflower and coat.

Place cauliflower on baking sheer lined with parchment. Bake for one hour, turning 3 times.
