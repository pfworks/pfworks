                Black Pepper Spice-Rubbed Beef Tenderloin

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/2      teaspoons  black peppercorn
  1 1/2      teaspoons  coriander seed
  1 1/2      teaspoons  cumin seed
  1 1/2      teaspoons  mustard seeds
     1/2      teaspoon  whole clove
     1/2      teaspoon  fennel seed
  4                     green cardamom pods -- crushed, reserving seeds and discarding pods
  1 1/2           inch  cinnamon stick -- broken into small pieces
  1 1/4                 coarse sea salt or kosher salt
  2             pounds  center-cut beef tenderloin -- trimmed and tied, room temp for 30 minutes
  1 1/2    tablespoons  vegetable oil

Toast spices until mustard seeds start to pop over med-low heat, 3-5 minutes. Cool and grind.

Preheat oven to 350 with rack in the middle.

Pat beef dry, then rub spice mixture all over it (including ends). Heat oil in same skillet over high heat until it shimmers. Brown beef on all sides, about 10 minutes total.

Transfer beef to a small roasting pan and roast until an instant-read thermometer register 120, 25 to 30 minutes. Transfer to a cutting board and let stand 10 minutes.

Cut off string, slice and serve.
