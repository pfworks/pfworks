                              Pollo Borracho

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  6                     boneless, skinless chicken breasts
     1/4           cup  lime juice
     1/4           cup  oil
  12            ounces  dark brewed Mexican Beer
  2        tablespoons  garlic puree
  2                     clove -- crushed
  1                     cardamom seed -- crushed
  1                     bay leaf
  6                     black peppercorn -- crushed
     1/2      teaspoon  salt

Rinse and pat dry chicken breasts, trimming fat. Combine marinade ingredients in a large bowl. Add chicken breasts, turning to coat well. Cover, refigerate and marinate at least 3 hours.

Remove chicken from marinade and pat dry.

Grill chicken over charcoal (2-5 minutes per side) until cooked through, brushing with marinade frequently. Slice and serve.
