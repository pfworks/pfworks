              Grilled Flank Steak with Bacon Balsamic Glaze

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  triple peppercorns
  1 1/4         pounds  flank steak
                        salt
  2          teaspoons  garlic flakes
  4             slices  bacon -- diced
     1/2           cup  balsamic vinegar
     1/2           cup  beef broth
  2          teaspoons  cornstarch
  2        tablespoons  parsley -- chopped

Heat a grill pan or grill to medium high heat. Coat pan with cooking spray. Crush peppercorns and season steak with peppercorns, salt and garlic.

Cook steak 5 minutes per side and let rest 10 minutes. Slice into 1/4" slices.

Place bacon in skillet over med heat until browned and crisp. Add vinegar and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Mix cornstarch and broth, whisking until completely dissolved. Add to skillet; simmer for 2 minutes to full boil until sauce thickens. Remove from heat. Spoon over steak with parsley.
