                          Fried Brussels Sprouts

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        vegetable oil
  1              pound  brussels sprout -- halved
                        kosher salt
  3                     shallots -- peeled and thinly sliced
  2                     fresno -- thinly sliced into rounds
  2        tablespoons  lime juice
  1         tablespoon  maple syrup or honey

Heat 2 inches oil in a heavy pot over med-high heat. When 360, add 1/3 of sprouts. Cook, turning for 2-3 minutes. Transfer to paper towel lined tray and sprinkle with salt. Fry in batches. Add shallots and chiles and fry for 1-2 minutes and place with sprouts.

Whisk lime juice and maple syrup in a large bowl. Add tray contents to coat. Season and serve.
