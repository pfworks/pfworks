              Yogurt Marinated Grilled Chicken with Harissa

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  whole milk yogurt
  1                 in  ginger -- peeled and grated
  1           teaspoon  ground cumin
  1           teaspoon  red chile flakes
                        kosher salt
  1                     whole chicken, cut in half
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  1                     lemon -- halved
                        harissa
  2          teaspoons  coriander seed
  1           teaspoon  cumin seed
  1           teaspoon  caraway seed
  2        tablespoons  paprika
  1           teaspoon  red chile flakes
  3             cloves  garlic -- peeled
  1                     red bell pepper -- roasted, peeled and seeded
                        sea salt
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil

In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger, cumin, chile flakes and salt. Whisk until smooth and pour into baking dish. Lay the chicken halves, skin side up, in the mixture. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for a couple of hours.

Preheat clean grill and oil when hot.

Remove chicken from yogurt and place on grill, skin side down. Cook until skin is brown and crispy and then flip. Grill slowly until 155, 35 to 40 minutes.

Remove chicken to cutting board. Cut and transfer to a serving platter and sprinkle with lemon juice. Top with harissa and serve,

For harissa: Preheat oven to 350.

In a small bowl, combine coriander seeds, cumin seeds, and caraway seeds. Add the spices to a sheet tray and toast in the oven for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from oven to cool. Add paprika and chile flakes to the mix.

Grind garlic into paste and add red bell pepper. Season with salt, and then add the spices and the 2 tablespoons of oil and blend well.
