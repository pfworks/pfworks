                           Green Chile Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  5             cloves  garlic -- minced
  16            ounces  frozen diced green chiles -- thawed
  2             medium  yellow onions -- chopped
  1 1/2      teaspoons  ground cumin
  1           teaspoon  chile powder
  1           teaspoon  oregano
  8                     chicken breast halves, skinless and boneless
  2 1/2           cups  monterey jack cheese -- shredded

Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease a baking dish.

In a mixing bowl, combine the garlic, green chiles, onion, cumin, chile powder and oregano. Mix well. Place chicken breasts in the prepared dish in a single layer. Pour sauce evenly over chicken. Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese, and bake an additional 5-10 minutes until cheese is melted. Serve.
