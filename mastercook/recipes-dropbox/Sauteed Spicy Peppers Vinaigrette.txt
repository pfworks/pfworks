                    Sauteed Spicy Peppers Vinaigrette

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4        tablespoons  virgin olive oil
  1             medium  red onion -- thinly sliced
  1              clove  garlic -- whole
  4               each  Italian frying peppers -- whole
  2               each  red bell pepper -- cored, seeded and cut into 1/2 in strips
  2               each  yellow bell pepper -- cored, seeded and cut into 1/2 in strips
  2              large  jalapeno pepper -- cored, seeded and julienned
  3        tablespoons  balsamic vinegar
  2        tablespoons  fresh marjoram leaves -- chopped
  6        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
                        salt
                        pepper

In a 10 or 12 in saute pan over med-high heat, heat virgin olive oil until smoking. Add onion and garlic clove until softened (4-5 minutes). Add all peppers, and, shaking regularly, cook until quite soft yet still retaining individual texture (10 -12 minutes). Remove from heat to large mixing bowl and let cool.

In small mixing bowl, combine balsamic vinegar, marjoram and extra virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Pour over cooled peppers and toss gently, being careful to not break peppers.v
