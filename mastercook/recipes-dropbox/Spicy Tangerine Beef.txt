                           Spicy Tangerine Beef

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3        tablespoons  soy sauce
  1         tablespoon  cornstarch
  1              pound  flank steak or tri-tip -- cut in thin strips
  2        tablespoons  dry sherry
  2        tablespoons  hoisin sauce
  2        tablespoons  honey
  1         tablespoon  chili sauce
  2        tablespoons  soy sauce
     1/4           cup  tangerine juice -- freshly squeezed
  3        tablespoons  canola oil
  2        tablespoons  fresh ginger -- minced
  3                     scallion -- chopped
     1/4                tangerine -- zested
  2        tablespoons  sesame seeds -- toasted

In a resealable plastic bag combine the soy sauce and cornstarch and mix well. Add beef, cover, and let marinate for 20 minutes in the refrigerator.

Whisk together the sherry, hoisin, chili sauce, soy sauce, and tangerine juice until completely combined.

In a large pan or wok, heat oil on high. Add the ginger and beef and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Then add sauce mixture and cook for another 2 minutes on medium heat until sauce thickens. Serve on warm platter, garnish with scallions, zest and sesame seds.
