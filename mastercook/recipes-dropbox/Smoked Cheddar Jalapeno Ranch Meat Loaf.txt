                 Smoked Cheddar Jalapeno Ranch Meat Loaf

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  applewood smoked bacon lardons -- 3/4" sticks
  1 3/4         pounds  ground premium beef sirloin
     3/4         pound  ground pork
  4              large  eggs
     1/4           cup  jalapeno -- minced
  1         tablespoon  worcestershire sauce
  1           teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
  2          teaspoons  salt
     1/3           cup  cilantro -- chopped
  1         tablespoon  toasted ground mexican oregano
     3/4           cup  cubed smoked cheddar
  1                cup  white onion -- diced

Preheat oven to 325. Partially cook the bacon in an ovenproof skillet over low heat for 5 minutes. Do not let the bacon get hard. Transfer to a large bowl, add all the other ingredients, and mix very well. Put the mixture back into the skillet or into a standard 8 inch loaf pan and bake for 1 hour, or until internal temperature is 150F.
