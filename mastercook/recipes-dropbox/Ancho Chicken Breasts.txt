                          Ancho Chicken Breasts

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2          teaspoons  shallot -- minced
  1           teaspoon  garlic -- minced
  2        tablespoons  ancho chile -- ground
  1                     juice of lime
  1           teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
  2          teaspoons  salt
     1/2           cup  olive oil
  4                     chicken breast -- skinless, boneless

Combine all ingredients, except chicken, in a small bowl. Place the chicken in a glass casserole dish and cover with marinade. Let the chicken stand for 2-3 hours, covered, in the refrigerator.

Cook breasts over a hot grill for 2 minutes per side or until done.
