                               French Toast

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1           teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/4      teaspoon  ground nutmeg
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  4        tablespoons  butter
  4                     eggs
     1/4           cup  milk
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla extract
  8             slices  challah, brioche or white bread
     1/2           cup  maple syrup -- warmed

Combine cinnamon, nutmeg and sugar.

In a 10-12 inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk and vanilla and pour into a shallow container. Dip bread in mixture. Fry until slices are golden brown, flip and cook other side. Serve with syrup.
