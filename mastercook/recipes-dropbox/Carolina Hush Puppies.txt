                          Carolina Hush Puppies

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  yellow cornmeal
  1                cup  flour
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  1         tablespoon  kosher salt
  4          teaspoons  baking powder
  1                cup  buttermilk
     1/4           cup  melted butter
     1/4      teaspoon  hot sauce
  1                     med yellow onion -- minced
                        canola oil

In a large bowk, whisk together cornmeal, flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder. In a medium bowk, whisk together buttermilk, butter, hot sauce, onion and 1/4 cup water. Add buttermilk mixture to dry ingredients and stir together with a spoon; let sit for 10 mintues.

Pour oil to a depth of 2" in a 6-qt Dutch oven and heat over medium high heat until a thermometer reads 375. Transfer batter to a piping bag with a 3/4" round tip. Working in batches, pipe and cut 3" long logs of batter into oil; fry until golden brown, 1-2 minutes. Transfer to paper towels to drain. Season with more salt.
