                            Spring Carrot Soup

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/4         pounds  carrot
  1         tablespoon  extra virgin olive oil
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  1              large  yellow onion -- chopped
  3               cups  vegetable stock
     1/2                juice of lemon
                        sea salt

Top the carrots and scrub them. Cut them into 1 inch segments. Heat the olive oil in a large, heavy soup pot over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and saute for a few minutes until the onions start to get translucent. Add the stock and carrots and bring to a gentle boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes until the carrots are tender. Remove from heat and cool for a few minutes. 

Puree. Stir in lemon juice and salt.
