                            Bourbon Pecan Pie

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3                     eggs -- lightly beaten
  1                cup  sugar
     1/2           cup  light corn syrup
     1/2           cup  dark corn syrup
     1/3           cup  butter -- melted
  2        tablespoons  bourbon
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  1                     unbaked 9 inch pie shell
  1 1/4           cups  pecans -- coarsely chopped

Preheat oven to 375. Mix together eggs, sugar, corn syrups, butter, bourbon, vanilla and salt until well blended.

Prick the sides and bottom of the pie shell with a fork at 1/2" intervals. Spread pecans on bottom and pour mixture over them. Bake for 35-45 minutes, until just set at edges but slightly loose in middle.

Place on a rack to cool.
