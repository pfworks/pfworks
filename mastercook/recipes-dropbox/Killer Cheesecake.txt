                            Killer Cheesecake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                     individual package graham crackers -- crushed
  2        tablespoons  white sugar
     1/2           cup  butter -- softened
  1 1/2      teaspoons  ground cinnamon
  24            ounces  cream cheese -- softened
  1                cup  white sugar
  3                     egg
  1 1/2      teaspoons  vanilla extract
  16            ounces  sour cream
  3          teaspoons  white sugar
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract

Preheat oven to 375.

In a food processor, combine crackers, 2 tbspns sugar, butter and cinnamon. Pulse until smooth. Press into the bottom of an 8x12 baking dish.

In the food processor, combine cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, eggs and vanilla. Process until smooth. Pour over crust.

Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until filling is set. Allow to cool for 2 hours.

Preheat oven to 350. In a small bowl, mix together sour cream, sugar and vanilla. Spread over top of cheesecake. Bake in oven for 10-15 minutes.
