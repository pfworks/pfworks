                  Ziti with Poblanos and Chipotle Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              large  onion -- thinly sliced
     1/2         pound  fresh poblabo -- thinly sliced lengthwise
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  1                cup  bottled roasted red pepper -- chopped
  1              pound  ziti or penne
  1         tablespoon  chipotle chile canned in adobo -- chopped
  1 1/2           cups  sour cream
  1                cup  scallion -- chopped
     1/4           cup  cilantro -- chopped

Cook onion, poblanos, and 1/2 tspn salt in oil in a heavy medium pot over medium heat, covered, stirring occasionally, until golden, 8 to 10 minutes/ Add red peppers and cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender, 2-3 minutes.

Meanwhile, cook ziti in a pasta pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup water then drain.

Puree chipotles with sour cream in a blender.

Add puree to onion mixture with scallions, cilantro, 1/4 cup cooking water, and 1/2 tspn salt and bring to a simmer, stirring. Add ziti and toss, moistening with additional water if desired. Season with salt.
