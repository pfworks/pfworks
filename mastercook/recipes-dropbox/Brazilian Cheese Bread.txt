                          Brazilian Cheese Bread

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/2           cups  parmesan cheese
  1 1/2           cups  corn oil
  1                cup  eggs (7-8)
  1 1/4           cups  manioc flour
  1 1/4           cups  sour manioc flour -- (or sub 2.5 cups tapioca for both flours)
  1                cup  2 percent milk
                        vegetable oil spray

Preheat oven to 350. Mix all ingredients with electric mixer and spray cupcake tray with vegetable oil.

Pour into cupcake pan, filling each mold to the top. Place in oven for 15 minutes until light brown.
