                         Cumin and Ancho Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             pounds  skinless boneless chicken thighs
  3        tablespoons  ancho chile powder
  3        tablespoons  ground cumin
  2          teaspoons  kosher salt
                        vegetable oil

Toss chicken with ancho chile powder, cumin and 2 teaspoons salt in a large bowl to coat. Cover and chill for 2 hours. Heat a large iron skillet over medium high heat. Coat pan with a thin layer of oil. Working in batches, place thighs in skillet in a single layer. Cover and cook until a crust forms, about 5 minutes. Turn; cook uncovered, until just cooked through, 3-4 minutes longer. Transfer to a platter, let rest for 5 minutes.
