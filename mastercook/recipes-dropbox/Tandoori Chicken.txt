                             Tandoori Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                cup  plain yogurt
     1/4           cup  fresh mint leaf -- chopped
     1/4           cup  fresh cilantro -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  vindaloo paste (Patak's)
  1           teaspoon  salt
     3/4      teaspoon  black pepper
  6              whole  chicken legs -- skin discarded

Put oven rack in middle position and preheat oven to 500. Line broiler pan with foil.

Stir together everything except chicken in a large bowl. Coat both sides of chicken legs well with all of mixture. Transfer to broiling pan in 1 layer.

Roast until charred in spots and cooked through, 25 to 30 minutes.
