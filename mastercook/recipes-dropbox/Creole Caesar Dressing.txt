                          Creole Caesar Dressing

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1           teaspoon  garlic -- minced
  1              large  egg
     2/3           cup  vegetable oil
     1/2           cup  extra virgin olive oil
  2        tablespoons  Parmesan cheese
  2          teaspoons  creole mustard
  2          teaspoons  lemon juice -- freshly squeezed
  2          teaspoons  lime juice -- freshly squeezed
  1           teaspoon  water
     1/8      teaspoon  angel dust cajun seasoning
     1/8      teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
     1/8      teaspoon  salt

Place garlic in food processor. Add egg, and with motor running, slowly add vegetable and olive oil. Ad cheese, mustard, lemon and lime juices, water, cajun seasoning, pepper and salt; Process briefly.
