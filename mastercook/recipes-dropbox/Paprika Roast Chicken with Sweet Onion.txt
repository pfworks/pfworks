                  Paprika Roast Chicken with Sweet Onion

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  extra virgin olive oil
  1 1/2    tablespoons  paprika
     1/2      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  cayenne
  1              whole  chicken -- cut into serving pieces
  1                     sweet onion -- 1/2" wedges

Preheat oven to 500 with rack in upper third.

Mix oil with spices, 1.5 tspns salt and 1 tspn pepper in a large bowl, then add chicken and onion, tossing to coat. Arrange chicken, skin side up, and onion, in a 3 quart shallow baking dish.

Bake until chicken is just cooked through and skin is golden, about 30 minutes. Skim any fat from pan juices in dish.
