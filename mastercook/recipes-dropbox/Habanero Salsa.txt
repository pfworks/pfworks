                              Habanero Salsa

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  onion -- finely chopped
  1 1/2           cups  Seville (bitter) orange juice
  2          teaspoons  mexican oregano
  1           teaspoon  salt
  1         tablespoon  habanero chile -- chopped and seeded

Stir together all ingredients in a bowl; let set for at least 30 minutes.

Yield:
  "2 cups"
