                        Four Flavor Sheet Pancakes

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 3/4           cups  buttermilk
  2              large  eggs -- beaten
  1 1/2      teaspoons  kosher salt
  1 1/2      teaspoons  vanilla extract
     1/3           cup  sugar
  2          teaspoons  sugar
  2 3/4           cups  flour
  1         tablespoon  baking powder
  1 1/2      teaspoons  baking soda
  4        tablespoons  butter -- melted
  2        tablespoons  strawberry jam -- warmed
     1/2           cup  strawberries -- sliced
     1/2           cup  chocolate chips
  1                     banana -- halved crosswise and thinly slices lengthwise
  2        tablespoons  light brown sugar
  1           teaspoon  cinnamon
  3        tablespoons  powdered sugar
                        maple syrup
                        whipped cream

Position oven rack in the center of the oven and preheat to 500. Whisk the buttermilk, eggs, salt, vanilla and 1/3 cup sugar together until combined. Whisk the flour, baking powder and baking soda in another large bowl. Fold buttermilk mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Reserve 2 tablespoons batter.

Brush 2 tablespoons butter on an 18x13" sheet pan and pour in batter, smoothing the top. Tap tray on counter to flatten.

Stir the warmed jam in a small bowl until pourable. Imagine the batter devided into 4 sections. Dollop jam over one section. Swirl and top with strawberries.

Sprinkle chocolate chips over 2nd section. Arrange banana in herringbone pattern over third section and sprinkle with sugar.

Mix brown sugar, cinnamon and reserved batter in a small bowl. Dollop about 1 teaspoon at a time, over last section and swirl.

Put the pan in oven and immediately reduce temperature to 400. Bake, rotating halfway through until golden brown, 18-23 minutes.

Brush with 2 tablespoons butter and let cool 15 minutes before cutting. Stir powdered sugar and 1 tspn water on bowl and drizzle over cinnamon section.
