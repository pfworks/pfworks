                      Quick Chinese-Style Vermicelli

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8             ounces  dried rice noodles
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil
  1              clove  garlic -- minced
  1         tablespoon  soy sauce
     1/2    tablespoon  chili sauce
                        salt and pepper
  1                     green onion -- chopped

Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook noodles for 4-5 minutes or until al dente. Drain.

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until tender. Stir in noodles and season with soy sauce, chili sauce, salt and pepper. Sprinkle top with green onions.
