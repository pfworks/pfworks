                               Eclair Cake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2                     3.4oz pkgs dry Vanilla Instant Pudding Mix
  3 1/2           cups  whole milk
  12            ounces  cool whip
  2                     14.4oz packages graham crackers
                        frosting
  6        tablespoons  butter
  6        tablespoons  milk
  6        tablespoons  cocoa
  1                cup  powdered sugar

Blend milk and vanilla pudding mix for about 2 minutes. Fold in cool whip.

In a 9x13 pan, layer graham crackers to cover bottom of pan.

Pour half of pudding/cool whip mixture on top and level.

Cover with another layer of crackers, and cover with rest of pudding.

Top with graham cracker layer bumpy side down.

Microwave butter and milk until just melted. Add cocoa and powdered sugar and blend. Pour over top.

Refrigerate overnight.
