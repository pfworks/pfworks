                       Gaucho Stuffed Rib-Eye Steak

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  olive oil
  12                    serrano pepper -- stems removed
  16                    garlic clove -- peeled
  4                     10-12 oz 1 rib eye steaks, at least 1 inch thick
                        salt
                        pepper
                        green chile paste

Heat oil in saucepan over moderate heat until hot. Add chiles and saute until the skins begin to brown, about 2 minutes. Remove with slotted spoon and place on paper towel. Add garlic and cook over low heat until soft, 4-6 minutes. Remove to paper towel and let cool.

Make 5 or 6 1 inch slits on the edge of each steak. Stuff each slit with a chile or garlic. Season all over with salt and pepper.

Grill steaks, or saute in a lightly oiled cast iron pan over high heat, 2-3 minutes per side.

Coat plates with paste and serve steaks.
