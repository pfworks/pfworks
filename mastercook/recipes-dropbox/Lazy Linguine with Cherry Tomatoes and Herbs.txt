               Lazy Linguine with Cherry Tomatoes and Herbs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  linguine
  1               pint  cherry tomato -- halved
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
     1/2           cup  basil -- roughly torn
     1/3           cup  flat leaf parsley -- finely chopped
  1         tablespoon  marjoram -- finely chopped
  1                     jalepeno -- finely chopped
     1/3           cup  extra virgin olive oil
                        kosher salt
                        freshly ground black pepper

In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta until al dente. Drain and cool under cold running water. Shake off excess water and pat dry with paper towels.

In a large bowl, toss the tomatoes with the garlic, basil, parsley, marjoram, jalepeno and olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Add pasta. Toss and serve.
