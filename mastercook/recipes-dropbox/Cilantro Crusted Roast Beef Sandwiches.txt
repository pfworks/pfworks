                  Cilantro Crusted Roast Beef Sandwiches

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        Roast
  2               cups  cilantro leaves
  6             cloves  garlic -- smashed
  1           teaspoon  celery seed
  2          teaspoons  coarse salt
     1/2      teaspoon  black pepper
  3             pounds  boneless rump roast -- trimmed
                        Cilantro Horseradish Pesto
  1              bunch  cilantro -- coarsely chopped
     1/4           cup  pine nut -- lightly toasted
  3             cloves  garlic -- coarsely chopped
  1           teaspoon  prepared horseradish
     1/2      teaspoon  coarse salt
     1/4           cup  extra virgin olive oil
  4                     kaiser or crusty sandwich rolls

Line a roasting pan with foil. Place beef on foil.

Preheat oven to 325.

In a food processor, combine cilantro, garlic, celery seed, salt and pepper. Puree until a smooth paste. Rub on roast, covering completely. Allow to marinate 10 minutes to 1 hour.

Roast the beef for about 1.5 hours for rare or until 125. Let rest 10 minutes and slice.

Combine dry pesto ingredients except oil. Puree until well combined. With motor running, drizzle in oil. Process until smooth.

To serve: Put a few slices of beed on each roll and drizzle pesto on top.
