                         Tricolor Roasted Peppers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2                     red bell pepper
  2                     yellow bell pepper
  2                     green bell pepper
  2                     poblano pepper
     1/2           cup  olive oil
     1/4           cup  balsamic vinegar
  3        tablespoons  fresh basil -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  fresh lemon juice
  2          teaspoons  minced garlic

Roast peppers. Peel, seed and cut into large triangles.

Mix all remaining ingredients in a large bowl. Add peppers and chiles to toss. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 3 hours. Bring to room temp and serve.
