                           Balsamic Roast Beef

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4             pounds  boneless roast beef
  1                cup  beef broth
     1/2           cup  balsamic vinegar
  1         tablespoon  worcestershire sauce
  1         tablespoon  soy sauce
  1         tablespoon  honey
     1/2      teaspoon  red pepper flakes
  4             cloves  garlic -- chopped

Place roast beef into slow cooker. In a 2-cup measuring cup, mix together remaining ingredients. Pour over beef and cook 4 hours on high or 6-8 on low.

Once cooked, remove from slow cooker onto platter. Break apart wirh forks and then ladle 1/2 cup gravy over beef.
