                             Peach BBQ Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  fresh peach
     3/4           cup  sweet onion -- chopped
  1 1/2    tablespoons  jalepeno -- minced w/ seeds
  1         tablespoon  canola oil
     1/4           cup  cider vinegar
     1/4           cup  bourbon
  2 1/2    tablespoons  mild honey
  2        tablespoons  dijon mustard
     3/4           cup  light brown sugar
     1/4      teaspoon  chili powder
     1/8      teaspoon  dry mustard
     1/4      teaspoon  kosher salt

Cut an X in the bottom of each peach, then blanch in a medium saucepan of boiling water for 10 seconds. Transfer with a slotted sppon to a bowl of ice water and cool. Peel peaches and coarsely chop.

Cook onion, jalepeno and a pinch of kosher salt n oil in a heavy medium saucepan over medium heat, stiring occasionally, until softened, 8 to 10 minutes. Add peaches and remaining ingredients and simmer, uncovered, stirring occassionally until peaches are very tender, about 30 minutes.

Put in a blender.

Yield:
  "2 cups"
