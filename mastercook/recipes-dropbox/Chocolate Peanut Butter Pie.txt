                       Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  25                    oreos
  4        tablespoons  butter -- melted
  1                cup  creamy peanut butter
  8             ounces  cream cheese -- softened
  1 1/4           cups  powdered sugar
  8             ounces  whipped topping such as cool whip

Preheat oven to 350. Crush cookies into crumbs. Pour melted butter over the top and stir with fork. Press into a pie pan and bake until set, 5-7 minutes. Remove from oven and cool completely.

Beat peanut butter with the cream cheese until smooth. Add powdered sugar and beat until smooth. Add whipped topping and beat until smooth.

Pour the filling to crust, evening out. Chill at least an hour.
