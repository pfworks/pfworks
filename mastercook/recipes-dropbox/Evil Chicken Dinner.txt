                           Evil Chicken Dinner

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  bacon -- cut into pieces
  1              pound  boneless skinless chicken breast -- chopped
  3        tablespoons  chili powder
  1                cup  brown sugar
     1/2           cup  water
                        cooked rice

Cook the bacon in a large skillet until nearly done. Don't drain. Stir in chicken and chili powder. Cook until cooked through. Add sugar and cook until dissolved and sauce begins to thicken, about 4-5 minutes. Add water if needed. Serve over rice.
