                 Baked Chicken with Chile Yogurt Marinade

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        Chile-Yogurt Marinade
  3                     garlic clove
  1                     jalepeno -- halved lenghtwise and seeded
  1              large  onion -- cut into large chunks
     1/4           cup  fresh cilantro, basil or parsley -- packed
  16            ounces  plain low-fat yogurt
                        grated zest of 2 limes
     1/4           cup  fresh lime juice
  2        tablespoons  chile powder
  1           teaspoon  salt
			
  4              large  chicken breast halves -- cut in half crosswise
  6                     chicken drumstick
  6                     chicken thigh

To make marinade, with machine running, drop the garlic and jalepeno through the feed tube of a food processor. Add the onion and cilantro, and pulse until the onion is finely chopped. Add the yogurt, lime zest and juice, chile powder and salt, and process into a puree. (or blend all but one cup of yogurt in blender and mix in remaining yogurt in a bowl)

Divide the chicken pieces between 2 1 gallon ziploc bags. Pour in marinade and seal bags. Refrigerate for 2-8 hours.

Position 2 racks in top third and center of oven and prehat to 400. Line two large baking sheets with lightly oiled foil.

Remove chicken from marinade. Arrange on baking sheets. Bake for 20 minutes. Turn chicken and switch sheet positions. Continue to bake about 25 minutes more. Serve hot.
