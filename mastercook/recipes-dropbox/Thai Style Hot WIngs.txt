                           Thai Style Hot WIngs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  curry powder
  2        tablespoons  sesame oil
  4             pounds  chicken wing
  2        tablespoons  ginger -- minced
  2        tablespoons  garlic -- minced
     1/4           cup  long thai chiles -- minced
     1/4           cup  lime juice
     1/4           cup  fish sauce
     1/4    tablespoon  sugar
     1/4           cup  chopped basil
     1/4           cup  chopped cilantro
     1/4           cup  chopped mint

In a bowl, combine curry powder, salt, pepper and sesame oil. Rub wings. Grill for 5-8 minutes, turning often until chicken is cooked. Remove wings to large bowl and toss with the rest of the ingredients.
