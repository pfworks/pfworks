                               PBC Mashers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4 1/2         pounds  red potato
  8        tablespoons  butter
     3/4      teaspoon  black pepper
  1 1/2      teaspoons  salt
     1/8           cup  sour cream
     1/8           cup  half and half
  1 1/2      teaspoons  granulated garlic

Wash and scrub potatoes (do not peel). Boil potatoes in a large pot of water until soft, about 20 minutes. Drain water and add butter, pepper, salt, sour cream, half and half and garlic. Mash until creamy and serve.
