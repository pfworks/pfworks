                          Snapple Lemon Iced Tea

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             quarts  water
  2                     Lipton tea bags
     3/4           cup  granulated sugar
     1/3           cup  bottled lemon juice
			
                        	

Bring water to a rapid boil in a large saucepan.

Turn off heat, add tea bags, cover saucepan and let steep for 1-2 hours.

Pour the sugar into a 2 qt pitcher, and then add the tea.

Add the lemon juice and chill
