                        Spicy Grilled Green Onions

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  red chile powder
  1                cup  olive oil
  3             cloves  garlic -- minced
  24             large  green onion

Combine all ingredients and marinate 4 hours or overnight.

Grill until all sides are browned.
