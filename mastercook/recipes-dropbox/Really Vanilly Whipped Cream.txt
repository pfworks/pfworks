                       Really Vanilly Whipped Cream

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                cup  heavy whipping cream
  2          teaspoons  pure vanilla extract
  2          teaspoons  confectioner's sugar

Beat the cream and vanilla in a chilled non-reactive bowl with a just until they hold a loose peak. Sift the sugar over the cream and continue to beat just until it holds a soft peak.

Yield:
  "2 1/2 cups"
