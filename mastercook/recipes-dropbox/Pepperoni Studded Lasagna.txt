                        Pepperoni Studded Lasagna

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             pounds  lasagna noodle
  2               cups  pepperoni -- 1/8" slices
  4               cups  tomato sauce, recipe follows
  1              pound  ricotta cheese
  16            ounces  shredded mozzarella cheese
  2             pounds  italian sausage -- cooked
     3/4           cup  grated parmesan cheese
                        tomato sauce
  3             ounces  extra virgin olive oil
  1                     yellow onion -- minced
  5             cloves  garlic -- crushed
  6               cups  roma tomato -- skinned and diced
  2        tablespoons  fresh basil leaves -- thinly sliced
  1         tablespoon  fresh oregano leaves -- minced
                        salt and pepper

In a medium saucepan, heat the olive oil. Add onion and cook over medium to low heat until transparent. Add garlic and cook until almost brown. Then add tomatoes and cook for 1/2 hour over low to medium heat. Add the basil and oregano and continue to cook for another 1/2 hour. Season to taste with salt and pepper, cool and store till ready to use.

Preheat oven to 375.

Boil 6 quarts of water, add pinch of salt and cook pasta to almost done. Remove from water and shock in ice bath.

In a medium saucepan add pepperoni and saute over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and drain on paper towel.

In a 10x14x3 inch baking pan or dish, pour 1 cup of tomato sauce in bottom and around sides. Layer lasagna sheets on the bottom of the pan, overlapping by 1/2 inch. Add 1/3 amount of ricotta, mozzarella and sausage. Sprinkle with parmesan. Add 1/2 cup sauce and 1/4 cup pepperoni. Repeat 2 more times.

On the top sheet, top with everything left and dust with parmesan. Bake for 45 minutes. Let cool for 15 minutes.
