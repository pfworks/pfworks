                    Grilled Pineapple Pork Sandwiches

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2         pounds  pork butt
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  1                     red onion -- diced
  2                     reb bell pepper -- diced
  2                     jalapeno -- diced
  1                cup  pineapple juice
     1/2           cup  soy sauce
  8                     King's Hawaiian sandwich buns
  1                     pineapple -- peeled and cored
     1/4           cup  cilantro or parsley

Place pork, garlic, onions, peppers, jalapeno, pineapple juice, and soy sauce in slow cooker. Cover and cook for 4-5 hours on high or 7-8 hours on low. Shred with forks.

Cover a baking sheet with foil and preheat oven to 450. Transfer meat to sheet, draining off extra juice into slow cooker. Arrange in an even layer and bake for 15 minutes. Remove and toss with a little extra juice.

Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cut pineapple into rings and cut rings in half. Grill until golden brown on skillet or grill. Put meat and pineapple in each bun, and garnish with cilantro or parsley.
