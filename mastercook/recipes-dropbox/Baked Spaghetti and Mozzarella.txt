                      Baked Spaghetti and Mozzarella

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        coarse salt
  56            ounces  whole peeled tomatoes
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  2             cloves  garlic -- smashed and peeled
     3/4         pound  spaghetti
  2               cups  packed basil leaves -- torn
     3/4         pound  fresh mozzarella -- 1/2" pieces

Preheat oven to 400. In a food processor, pulse tomatoes until coarsely chopped. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook for about 1 minute. Add tomatoes and bring to a boil. Cook at a rapid simmer until sauce thickens, about 12 minutes. Cook pasta for 5 minutes. Drain and return to pot. Add tomato sauce, basil and half the mozzarella and toss to combine. Transfer to 3qt baking dish and top with remaining cheese. Bake until golden and bubbly, about 25 minutes.
