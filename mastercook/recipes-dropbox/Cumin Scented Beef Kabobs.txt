                        Cumin Scented Beef Kabobs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  olive oil
  2        tablespoons  oregano -- finely chopped
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  2          teaspoons  ground cumin
  1           teaspoon  ground coriander
     1/4      teaspoon  cayenne
  2             pounds  flatiron steak -- 1.5" cubes

Stir together oil, oregano, garlic, spices and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl, then toss with beef. Marinate, chilled, at least 2 hours.

Prepare grill for direct heat cooking over medium hot charcoal. Thread beed on skewers.

Oil grill rack, then grill 4-5 minutes, turning.
