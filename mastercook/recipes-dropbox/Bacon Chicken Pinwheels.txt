                         Bacon Chicken Pinwheels

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  bonesless skinless chicken breast
  8             slices  thick pepper bacon
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  2          teaspoons  dried thyme
  1           teaspoon  garlic powder

Slice chicken into 1 inch thick slices.

Slightly cook bacon.

Divide strips into 4 equal amounts.

Lay a strip of bacon on top of each chicken slice and roll up with the bacon on the inside. You will probably use 2-3 strips of chicken and 2 slices of bacon.

Use toothpicks to hold together.

Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with thyme and garlic powder.

Grill over medium heat until chicken is completely cooked, usually about 5 minutes per side.
