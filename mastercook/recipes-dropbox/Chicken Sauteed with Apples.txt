                       Chicken Sauteed with Apples

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4                     boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  1                     firm apple -- cored, halved and 1/2" slices
  1                cup  apple juice
  1              large  onion -- thinly sliced
  1              clove  garlic -- minced
     1/2      teaspoon  dried thyme
                        salt and pepper
  2        tablespoons  dijon mustard

Pound chicken to 3/4".

Heat oil in skillet over med-high heat. Saute chicken until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.

Add apple slices, apple juice, onion, garlic, thyme, 1/2 tspn salt and 1/8 tspn pepper Simmer, covered, 6-8 minutes or until fork tender.

Remove chicken, apple and onion to a platter and keep warm.

Bring sauce to boil for about 5 minutes. Whisk in mustard. Pour sauce over chicken.
