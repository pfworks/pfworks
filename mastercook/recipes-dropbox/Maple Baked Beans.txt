                            Maple Baked Beans

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  dried navy beans -- picked over and rinsed
  1                     onion -- chopped
  1              large  garlic clove -- chopped fine
     1/2         pound  bacon -- chopped
  8               cups  water
     1/2           cup  grade B maple syrup or grade A with 3 drops maple extract added to taste.
  1           teaspoon  english-style dry mustard
     1/2      teaspoon  paprika

In a 7.5 qt ovenproof heavy kettle measuring about 12 inches across combine beans, onion, garlic, bacon and water. Simmer, covered partially, for one hour. Ladle out and reserve 1/2 cup liquid.

Preheat oven to 300.

In a bowl stir together reserved liquid, mape syrup, mustard and paprika until combined well and stir into bean mixture with enough additional water to just cover beans.

Bake beans, covered, adding water at hourly intervals to keep beans covered for 5 hours.
