                               Orange Pasta

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8             ounces  rigatoni or penne
  3        tablespoons  olive oil
  4             ounces  bacon -- chopped
  1             medium  onion -- thinly sliced
  3             cloves  garlic -- thinly sliced
  1                cup  orange juice
     1/2           cup  tomato paste
                        salt and pepper
     1/3           cup  heavy cream
     1/4           cup  parmesan cheese -- grated
                        zest of 1/2 orange
     1/2           cup  basil -- julienned

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and add pasta. Heat skillet over medium heat and add olive oil and bacon. Allow to crisp, then add onion and garlic. Cook for about 10 minutes until carmelized.

Stir in orane juice and tomato paste, and bring to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper, and add cream. Scoop in pasta and cheese. Stir. Add pasta water to thin if needed.

Stir in orange zest and basil and serve.
