                    Jerk Marinated roasted drumsticks

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8                     chicken drumstick
  1 1/2           cups  Jamaican Jerk Marinade
                        cilantro

Rinse drumsticks and pat dry. Place drumsticks in a resealable plastic bag; add marinade. Refrigerate overnight or up to 24 hours.

Heat oven and roasting pan to 500. emove drumsticks from marinade; place in roasting pan. Roast 25 to 35 minutes or until drumsticks are no longer pink in center.
