                             Sugar Cream Pie

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  heavy cream
     1/2           cup  all-purpose flour
     1/2           cup  brown sugar
     1/2           cup  sugar
  3        tablespoons  sugar
     1/2           cup  whole milk
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
  9                 in  piecrust
  1         tablespoon  unsalted butter
  1           teaspoon  ground cinnamon

Preheat oven to 350. In a medium bowl, combine cream, flour, brown sugar, 1/2 cup sugar, milk and vanilla.

Fit crust into 9 inch pie pan and dot bottom with butter. Pour filling into crust. Combine cinnamon and 3 tbs sugar and sprinkle on top. Bake until set and firm to the touch, about 1 hour. Cool in wire rack.
