            Baked Cauliflower Bites with Cheddar Dipping Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 3     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  5               cups  cauliflower florers
     1/2           cup  panko crumbs
     1/4           cup  seasoned bread crumbs
  2        tablespoons  parmesan cheese
  1           teaspoon  italian seasoning
                        salt and pepper
  4                     egg white
  2        tablespoons  dijon mustard
  3        tablespoons  water
                        sauce
     1/2           cup  milk
  3        tablespoons  dijon mustard
  1           teaspoon  whole wheat flour
  4             ounces  cheddar cheese -- shredded
  1           teaspoon  parsley -- chopped

Preheat oven to 425. Lightly coat a large baking sheet with spray. 

In a wide, shallow dish whisk the egg whiyes, mustard and water and a couple pinches of salt and pepper together. In a separate, wide, shallow dish combine panko, bread crumbs, parmesan and Italian seasoning.

Dip the florets one at a time bu holding onto the stalk first into the egg mixture allowing excess to drip off then into the bread crumbs twirling to coat evenly all the way around. Place on baking sheet. Repear until all are coated. 

Bake 20-26 minutes until tender and lightly golden.

Whisk milk, mustard and lour in a small sauce pan over med-high heat; bring to a simmer, whisking often. Reduce heat to low, stir in cheddar cheese and parsley and continue stirring until cheese is melted and mixture is smooth. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper.
