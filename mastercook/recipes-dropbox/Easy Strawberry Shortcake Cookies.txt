                    Easy Strawberry Shortcake Cookies

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     3/4           cup  strawberries -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  lemon juice
  4             ounces  cream cheese -- softened
     1/4           cup  butter -- softened
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
     3/4           cup  sugar
  1                     egg
  1 1/4           cups  all purpose flour
     1/2      teaspoon  baking powder
     1/8      teaspoon  salt
  6             ounces  white chocolate chip
  2        tablespoons  flour

Preheat oven to 350 and line cookie sheets with parchment.

Squeeze lemon juice over the strawberries.

In a large bowl, cream together the cream cheese, butter, vanilla and sugar. Add egg and mix until well combined.

Whisk flour, baking powder and salt. Gradually add flour to the wet mixture and mix untill well combined.

Add white chocolate and mix.

Mix 2 tablespoons of flour with strawberries and add to cookie mix and combine.

Scoop cookies onto cookie sheet and bake for 13-15 minutes, until golden brown.

Transfer to rack to cool completely.

Yield:
  "3 dozen"
