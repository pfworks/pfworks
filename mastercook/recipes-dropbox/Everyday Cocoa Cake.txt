                           Everyday Cocoa Cake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  all-purpose flour
     3/4           cup  unsweetened cocoa powder
  1 1/4      teaspoons  baking soda
     3/4      teaspoon  salt
     3/4           cup  unsalted butter -- softened
  1 3/4           cups  light brown sugar -- packed
  2              large  eggs
  1           teaspoon  vanilla
  1 1/3           cups  water
                        powdered sugar for dusting

Preheat oven to 350. Butter a 9x2 inch round cake pan and dust with flour, knocking out excess.

Whisk together flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt.

Beat together butter and brown sugar with an electric mixer until pale and fluffy. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each additino, then beat in vanilla.

Add flour mixture and water alternately in batches, beginning and ending with flour and mixing until just combined.

Pour batter into cake pan and bake in middle of oven until springy and tester comes out clean, 55 minutes to 1 hour. Cool in pan 1 hour. Invert onto a rack, invert again and dust with powdered sugar.
