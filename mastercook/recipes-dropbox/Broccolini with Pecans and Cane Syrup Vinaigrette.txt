            Broccolini with Pecans and Cane Syrup Vinaigrette

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  kosher salt
  3            bunches  fresh broccolini
                        Cane Syrup Vinaigrette
  1                cup  toasted chopped pecans

Bring salt an 8 qt water to boil. Cook broccolini for 3-4 minutes. Drain and transfer to larger bowl.

Toss broccolini and 2 Tbsp Cane Syrup Vinaigrette. Top with pecans and serve with remaining vinaigrette.
