                           Hot Shredded Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3                     jalepeno chiles -- coarsely chopped
  1 1/2           cups  evaporated milk
  4             slices  fresh white bread -- coarsely torn
     1/2           cup  walnuts
  3        tablespoons  salad oil
  1              small  red onion -- finely diced
  1           teaspoon  fresh garlic -- minced
  1           teaspoon  fresh oregano -- chopped
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  black pepper -- freshly ground
  1              whole  chicken -- cooked, skin and bones removed and shredded
     1/2           cup  parmesan cheese -- freshly grated

In a blender place the jalepeno peppers, evaporated milk, bread and walnuts. Puree until smooth and set aside.

In a large saucepan place the oil and heat it on med high until hot. Add red onions, garlic, oregano, salt and black pepper. Saute for 4-6 minutes, or until the onions are tender.

Lower the heat to medium. Add the pureed mixture to the onions and stir well. Cook the mixture for 5-7 minutes or until it is thick and creamy, stirring occasionally.

Add the shredded chicken and stir it in.

Add the parmesan cheese and stir it in.
