                         Beef Stew in Honey Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 5     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  flour
  1           teaspoon  ginger -- ground
     1/4      teaspoon  black pepper -- ground
  2             pounds  boneless lean beef chuck roast -- 1.5" cubes
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  2               cups  baby carrot
  4               cups  beef broth
  24                    pearl onion -- peeled
  2        tablespoons  honey
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  2               cups  potato -- diced

heat oven to 400. In large plastic bag combine flour ginger and pepper, shake well. Add beef and shake to coat.

Heat oil in large oven proof pot over med-high heat. Add beef in batches, 6-8 minutes until browned.

Add carrots and 2 cups broth. Cover with foil then lid. Bake 1.5 hours.

Add onions, and 1/2 cup of broth if dry. Bake 20-25 minutes.

With slotted spoon, transfer beef, carrots and onions to a bowl. Keep warm. Place pot on top of stove. Skim off fat. Bring juices to a bowl over med-high heat. Add remaining broth honey and salt. Cook 5-10 minutes, until sauce slightly thickens, stirring occassionally.

Return beef to pot, mix well. Cook until heated.
