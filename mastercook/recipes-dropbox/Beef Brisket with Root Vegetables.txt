                    Beef Brisket with Root Vegetables

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  flour
  4             pounds  first cut brisket, trimmed
  1                cup  meatless marinara sauce
     1/2           cup  beer
  3                     bay leaf
  3             medium  onion -- quartered
  3              large  carrots -- 1" pieces
  2              large  potato -- 1.5" pieces
  1              large  parsnip -- 1/2" pieces
  6                     garlic clove -- halved
     1/8      teaspoon  salt
     1/8      teaspoon  freshly ground pepper

Heat oven to 325. Dust inside of large roasting bag with flour; set in roasting pan. Place brisket in bag.

In large bowl, combine marinara sauce, beer and bay leaves; mix well. Pour over meat, tilting bag to coat underside of meat.

Arrange onions, carrots, potatoes, parsnip and garlic around meat, spooning some of the sauce onto veges.

Tie bag closed. Cut 3 or 4 1/2" slits in bag.

Cook 2.5-3 hours or until internal temp reaches 160.

Let rest 15 minutes before slicing. Discard bay leaves.

Skim fat from juices. Season with salt and pepper. Drizzle juices over roast. Pour remainder into gravy boat.
