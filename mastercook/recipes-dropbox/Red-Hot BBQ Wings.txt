                            Red-Hot BBQ Wings

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             pounds  wings
     1/4           cup  butter
  5        tablespoons  Durkee Red Hot Sauce
  2        tablespoons  red wine vinegar
     1/4      teaspoon  garlic powder
  1         tablespoon  BBQ sauce

Slowly melt butter in a large saucepan. Add Durkee sauce, vinegar, garlic powder and bbq sauce. Remove from heat.

Heat oil in large frying pan to about 370. Deep fry wings, a few at a time until nicely browned and crisp, about 10 to 15 minutes. Remove to a paper-towel lined plate to drain.

Reheat sauce and add wings to saucepan. Toss to coat and transfer to platter.
