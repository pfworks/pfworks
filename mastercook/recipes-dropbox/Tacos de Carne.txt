                              Tacos de Carne

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  oil
     1/2           cup  flour
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  black pepper
     1/4           cup  garlic puree
  1               each  egg
  1              pound  lean ground beef
  12                    corn tortilla

Heat oil in large frying pan or deep fryer to 375. Work flour, salt, pepper, garlic puree and egg into beef. Warm tortillas. Spread 2 tablespoons beef mixture on half of tortilla. Fold over, press and secure with toothpick.  Fry in hot oil until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Remove pick and open up and stuff with cheese and stuff.
