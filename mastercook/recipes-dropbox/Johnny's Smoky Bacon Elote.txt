                        Johnny's Smoky Bacon Elote

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  5             slices  bacon
     1/2           cup  mayonnaise
  3                ear  corn
     1/4           cup  parmesan cheese
                        hot sauce
  1                     lime

In a skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon until crispy. Set aside and add rendered fat to mayonnaise. Grill corn until bright, plump and starts to char.

Remove from grill. Brush with mayo, dust with parmesan, drizzle with hot sauce, top with crumbled bacon, and squeeze a bit of lime.
