                          Maple Vanilla Carrots

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  carrots -- peeled and cut into 1/2" diagonal slices
  2               cups  water
  1                     vanilla bean -- halved lenghtwise
  2        tablespoons  pure maple syrup
  1         tablespoon  unsalted butter
     1/2      teaspoon  coarse salt
  1              pinch  ground black pepper

Combine carrots and water in a large skillet. Using tip of a paring knife, scrape vanilla seeds into skillet; add pod. Bring to a simmer, and cook until carrots are tender and most of the water has evaporated, about 10 minutes. Add syrup, salt, and pepper and toss.
