                                Pecan Pie

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2       package  refrigerated pie crust
  3              large  egg
     1/2           cup  sugar
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  3        tablespoons  butter -- melted
  1                cup  dark corn syrup
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
  2               cups  pecan half



Fir piecrust into a 9 inch pieplate according to directions. Fold edges under, and crimp.

Whisk together eggs and next 5 ingredients until thoroughly blended. Stir in pecans. Pour mixture into piecrust.

Bake at 350 on lower rack 40 minutes or until pie is set, shielding edges with foil after 15 minutes. Cool completely on wire rack.
