                        Slow Cooker Butter Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  4             cloves  garlic -- crushed
  1                     onion -- diced
  14            ounces  light coconut milk
  6             ounces  tomato paste
  2        tablespoons  whole wheat flour
  2          teaspoons  garam masala
  1           teaspoon  curry powder
     1/2      teaspoon  chile powder
     1/2      teaspoon  ginger powder
                        kosher salt and pepper
  3             pounds  boneless, skinless chicken breasts -- cut into 2" pieces
  2        tablespoons  cilantro -- chopped

Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium high heat. Add garlic and onion, and cook, stirring frequently, until onions have become translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Stir in coconut milk, tomato paste, flour, garam masala, curry powder, chile powder and ginger powder until well combined and slightly thickened, about 1-2 minutes; season with salt and pepper.

Place chicken in slow cooker, cover with sauce and toss. Cover and cook on low for 5 hours.

Servea and garnish with cilantro.
