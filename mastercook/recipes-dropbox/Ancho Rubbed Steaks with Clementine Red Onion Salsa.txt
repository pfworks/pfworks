           Ancho Rubbed Steaks with Clementine Red Onion Salsa

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                cup  peeled clementimes
     1/2           cup  red onion -- chopped
     1/2           cup  fresh cilantro -- chopped
  2 1/2    tablespoons  olive oil
  2          teaspoons  white wine vinegar
  2 1/2      teaspoons  ancho chile powder
     1/8      teaspoon  cayenne pepper
  2         3/4" thick  new york steaks

Mix clementines, red onion, cilanto, 1.5 tablespoons olive oil, and white wine vinegar in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper.

Combine chile powder and cayenne and sprinkle over steaks with salt on both sides. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in skillet over med-hight heat. Cook to desired doneness, 3-4 minutes per side for medium rare. Serve with salsa.
