                            Carrot-Cumin Salad

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  8                     carrots -- peeled and grated
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  1            squeeze  lemon
  1           teaspoon  whole cumin seeds

Combine the carrots in a bowl with the salt, olive oil and lemon. Toast the cumin seeds until fragrant and grind. Add to carrots and toss. Serve.
