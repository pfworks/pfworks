                           Roasted Tomato Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2         pounds  plum tomato -- quartered
  1                     yellow onion -- diced
  1                     green bell pepper -- 1" pieces
  1                     red bell pepper -- 1" pieces
     1/2           cup  extra virgin olive oil
  1 1/2      teaspoons  kosher salt
     1/4      teaspoon  crushed red pepper
     1/4           cup  fresh basil -- chopped
  2          teaspoons  fresh oregano

Preheat oven to 375. In a 9x13 baking pan, toss together tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, oil, salt and crushed red pepper. Roast for 1 hour. Remove pan from oven and fold basil and oregano into vegetables. Cook for 30 minutes more.

In a food processor, blend vegetables until pureed.

Yield:
  "3 cups"
