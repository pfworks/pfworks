                     Disneyland Hand Dipped Corn Dogs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              quart  vegetable oil for frying
  1                cup  corn meal
  1                cup  flour
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  2          teaspoons  baking powder
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
  1              large  egg
  1 1/4           cups  buttermilk
  2        tablespoons  melted butter
  8                     hot dogs

Heat oil to 350.

Stir together cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Whisk in egg, milk and butter.

Dip each dog into batter. Place into fryer, 3-4 minutes per corndog until golden.
