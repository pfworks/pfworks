                           French Toast Souffle

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/3           cup  maple syrup
  2              large  eggs
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla extract
  2        tablespoons  all-purpose flour
     1/8      teaspoon  kosher salt
                        powdered sugar

Preheat oven to 400 with rack in lowest third. Place 4 5oz ramekins on baking sheet and spray with oil.

Beat syrup, egg yolks, and vanilla in a medium bowl with an electric mixer on med-high until combined, about 1 minute. Add flour and mix.

Beat egg whites and salt until stiff peaks form. Gently fold 1/4 into syrup mixture. Fold this back into meringue. Pour into ramekins.

Place in oven, reduce temperatire to 375 and bake until puffed and golden, 12-13 minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve immediately.
