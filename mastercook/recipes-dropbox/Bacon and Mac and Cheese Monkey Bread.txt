                  Bacon and Mac and Cheese Monkey Bread

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  12       tablespoons  butter -- melted
  32            ounces  refrigerated biscuit dough
  3 1/2           cups  cheddar cheese -- shredded
  1                     packet powdered cheese mix from mac and cheese
  6             slices  cooked bacon -- crumbled

Position a rack in the lower third of the oven and preheat to 350. Put butter in a medium bowl. Brush pan with some of butter.

Cut biscuits into quarters. Combine cheddar and cheese mix in a large bowl and stir.

Yield:
  "12 "
