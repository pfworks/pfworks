                          Farmer's Market Salsa

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         blenderful  nice, ripe, garden tomatoes
  1             medium  sweet onion
  3             cloves  garlic
  6                     fresh basil leaf
  1              pinch  oregano
  3                     mild green chiles -- seeded
  3                     roasted green chiles -- seeded and peeled
  1              pinch  paprika
  1           teaspoon  parsley
  10             drops  red wine vinegar
                        salt and pepper

Blend tomatoes, onions and chopped garlic with basil. Add oregano, salt and pepper. Boil this mixture for 10 minutes, until it thickens. Add and stir chopped fresh green chiles, leaving a few seeds. Cook 5 minutes. Add chopped roasted chiles and paprika. One second before removing from stove, add finely chopped parsley and wine vinegar.
