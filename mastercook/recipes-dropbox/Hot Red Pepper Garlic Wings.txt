                       Hot Red Pepper Garlic Wings

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2         pounds  wings
     1/2           cup  butter -- melted
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  3          teaspoons  chile powder
  1              small  onion -- chopped
  4        tablespoons  cilantro -- chopped
  3              large  garlic clove -- chopped
     1/4      teaspoon  salt

Puree basting ingredients in a food processor.

Place wings in a large bowl, pour puree over wings, stir to coat evenly.

Preheat broiler. Place wings on broiling rack and broil 15-20 minutes or until cooked through. Turn after 10 minutes and baste.
