                             Bourbon Chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  butter
  2        tablespoons  peanut oil
  3 1/2         pounds  chicken -- cut into pieces
     1/2           cup  bourbon
  1             medium  onion -- finely chopped
     1/2         small  red bell pepper -- seeded and chopped
     1/2      teaspoon  dried thyme -- crumbled
     1/2      teaspoon  dried rosemary -- crumbled
                        salt and pepper
     1/2           cup  chicken broth
     1/4           cup  heavy cream

In a large, heavy skillet, heat the butter and oil over moderate heat, add the chicken pieces, and brown on all sides. Add 1/4 cup of bourbon, ignite with match, and burn till flames die. Add onion, bell pepper, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring, till softened, about 5 minutes. Add remaining 1/4 cup bourbon and broth, bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer till chicken is tender, 30-40 mnutes.

With a slotted spoon, transfer the chicken and vegetables to a serving platter. Add cream to skillet, return the heat to moderate, and cook, stirring, till thickened, about 3-5 minutes. Pour over chicken.
