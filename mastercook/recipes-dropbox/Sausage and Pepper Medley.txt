                        Sausage and Pepper Medley

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  20            ounces  Italian Links
     1/4           cup  olive oil
  2             cloves  garlic -- crushed
  1                     red bell pepper -- julienned
  1                     yellow bell pepper -- julienned
  1                     orange bell pepper -- julienned
     1/2                yellow onion -- julienned
  28            ounces  can of chopped italian tomatoes with herbs
                        salt
                        freshly ground black pepper
     1/2           cup  parmesan cheese -- grated

Prepare links and bias slice; set aside.

In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, and saute for 30 seconds. Add the peppers and onion and saute until tender-crisp, about 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes and sausage and cook for 5 minues. Season to taste and sprinkle with cheese.
