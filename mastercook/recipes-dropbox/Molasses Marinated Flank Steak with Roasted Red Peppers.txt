         Molasses Marinated Flank Steak with Roasted Red Peppers

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  canola oil
     1/2           cup  cola
  1         tablespoon  molasses
  1         tablespoon  soy sauce
  1         tablespoon  red pepper flakes
  2        tablespoons  minced garlic
  1         tablespoon  freshly cracked black pepper
  1                     lime -- juiced
  2             pounds  flank steak -- trimmed
  1                     baguette -- cut on the bias into 1/2" thick slices
     1/2           cup  extra virgin olive oil
  8             ounces  herb seasoned cream cheese
  10            ounces  jarred red pepper strips
  3        tablespoons  chopped cilantro leaves

In a small bowl mix together the oil, cola, molasses, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, garlic, black pepper and lime juice. Put the steak into a large shallow bowl and pour in the marinade. Move the steak around to assure the marinade is covering the meat. Allow to marinate 2 hours or up to 4 hours in the refirigerator.

Preheat grill over medium heat.

Remove the steak from the marinade and put it on the grtill. Cook for 4 minutes on each side. Remove from the grill to a cutting board and let rest 5 minutes.

Brush the break slices with cream cheese. Slice the steak against the grain into very thin strips. Top the cheese with a strip of meat and a red pepper strip. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
