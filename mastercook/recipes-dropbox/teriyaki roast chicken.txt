                          teriyaki roast chicken

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1               3 lb  broiler-fryer
  1              small  onion -- quartered
     1/3           cup  teriyaki sauce
  1           teaspoon  garlic pepper seasoning
                        cookin spray

remove giblets. rinse and drain chicken, pat dry.

Place onion in chicken cavity. Brush chicken with t-sauce. Sprinkle with seasoning. Put chicken breast side up in rack, pour remaining t-suace over chicken.

bake uncovered at 375 for 1.5 hours.

remove skin
