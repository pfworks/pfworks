                  Sour Cream Maple Cake with Lemon Glaze

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        cake
                        nonstick vegetable oil spray
  2               cups  all-purpose flour
  22         teaspoons  baking powder
     1/2      teaspoon  baking soda
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  1 1/3           cups  sour cream
     1/4           cup  pure maple syrup
     1/2           cup  walnuts -- toasted and chopped
     1/4           cup  maple sugar
  1 1/3           cups  maple sugar
     1/4      teaspoon  ground cinnamon
  13       tablespoons  butter -- cut into pieces and room temp
  2              large  egg
  2          teaspoons  vanilla extract
                        glaze
  1 1/3           cups  powdered sugar
  3        tablespoons  sour cream
  1         tablespoon  pure maple syrup
  1 1/2      teaspoons  fresh lemon juice
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
     1/2      teaspoon  grated lemon peel
                        whipped cream

Preheat oven to 350. Butter 9 inch bundt pan, then spray with nonstick spray. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in mediumm bowl. Whisk sour cream and maple syrup in another medium bowl. Mix waalnuts, 1/4 cup maple syrup, and cinnamon  in small bowl.

Using electric mixer, beat butter and remaining 1 1/3 cups maple sugar in large bowll until fluffy. Gradually beat in eggs and vanilla, occasionally stopping to scrape down sides of bowl. Add flour mixture in 3 additioons, alternating with sour cream mixture in 2 additions. Transfer 2/3 of bater to pan. Sprinkle with walnut mixture. Pour remaining butter into pan.

Bake until done, about 55 minutes. Cool 20 minutes. Turn onto rack and cool completely.

Glaze: Whisk first 6 ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Let stand until thickened, about 30 minutes. Pour over cake.
