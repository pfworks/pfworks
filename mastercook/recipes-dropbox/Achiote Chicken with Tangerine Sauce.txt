                   Achiote Chicken with Tangerine Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  achiote paste
  1         tablespoon  honey
  1         tablespoon  red wine vinegar
  1         tablespoon  tangerine peel -- grated
  2             cloves  garlic
  1           teaspoon  cumin seed
     1/2      teaspoon  ground cinnamon
  4                     skinless boneless chicken breast halves
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  1                cup  fresh tangerine juice
                        cilantro -- chopped

Blend first 7 ingredients in processor to form paste. Place chicken in square baking dish. Spread marinade over, turning to coat. Cover; refrigerate 2 to 4 hours.

Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle chicken with salt. Add chicken to skillet; cook and brown, about 2 minutes per side. Add 1/2 cup tangerine juice to skillet. Cover; reduce heat to medium and simmer until chicken is cooked through, turning once, about 5 minutes. Transfer chicken to plates. Add remaining 1/2 cup juice to skillet; boil until thickened, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon sauce over chicken. Sprinkle with cilantro.
