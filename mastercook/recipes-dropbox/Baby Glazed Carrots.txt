                           Baby Glazed Carrots

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  baby carrot
  3        tablespoons  unsalted butter
     1/4           cup  water
     1/4           cup  maple syrup
  2        tablespoons  orange juice
     1/2      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/4      teaspoon  ground allspice
     1/4      teaspoon  salt

Place everything in a medium, heavy saucepan.

Bring to a boil over medium high heat, stirring occasionally, until carrots are tender and the sauce becomes a shiny glaze, about 15 minutes.

Serve
