                          Mini Deep Dish Pizzas

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        olive oil
                        all-purpose flour
     1/2         pound  pizza dough -- home made or store bought, in 6 pieces
                        coarse salt and ground pepper
     1/2           cup  mozzarella cheese -- shredded
  1              large  tomato -- coarsely chopped
                        toppings

Preheat oven to 450. Lightly brush 6 standard muffin cups with oil. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each dough piece to a 6 inch round. Fill each cup with 1 round, gently pressing dough into the bottom and sides of the cup. Season with salt andpepper.

Sprinkle each dough cup with 1 tbspn each cheese and tomato. Add desired toppings and another tbspn each cheese and tomato. Bake until dough is golden brown and crisp, 12 minutes. Let cool 2 minutes before removing from cups.
