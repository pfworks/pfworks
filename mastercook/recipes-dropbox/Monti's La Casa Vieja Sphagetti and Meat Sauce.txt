              Monti's La Casa Vieja Sphagetti and Meat Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  2             pounds  ground beef
     1/3           cup  sweet dried basil
  1              clove  garlic -- minced
     1/2           cup  red bell pepper -- chopped
     1/2           cup  onion -- chopped
     1/2           cup  celery -- chopped
     1/2           cup  carrot -- chopped
  16            ounces  tomato paste
  16            ounces  whole tomatoes
     1/4         pound  salt pork -- diced
                        salt and pepper
  1              pound  spaghetti -- cooked and drained

Heat oil over medium to high heat in a large heavy skillet or saucepan. Add ground beef and cook until meat is brown. Drain beef well, return to skillet, and stir in remaining ingredients. Simmer for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally, until sauce has thickened and flavors are well blended. Prepare pasta. Pour sauce over pasta and serve.
