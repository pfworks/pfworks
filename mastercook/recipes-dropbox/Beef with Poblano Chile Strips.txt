                      Beef with Poblano Chile Strips

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  butter
  1         tablespoon  corn oil
  1              pound  beef filet or sirloin -- cut into strips
  1             medium  white onion -- cut into crescents
  4                     poblano chiles -- roasted, peeled, seeded and torn into strips
  1              small  red bell pepper -- seeded and cut into strips
  1                cup  heavy cream

Melt the butter with the oil in a large skillet. Saute beef strips over high heat until they brown. Add the onion and continue cooking until transparent. Add the poblano and red pepper and cook for 3 minutes. Add the cream and salt and cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring constantly. Serve immediately.
