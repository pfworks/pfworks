                             Rojo Rico Salsa

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  caribe
  2        tablespoons  red chile powder
  1                cup  boiling water
  12              each  red chile pods
  4             cloves  garlic
     1/4      teaspoon  cumin
     1/2      teaspoon  oregano
                        salt

To the caribe and red chile powder, add the cup of boiling water and let stand. Clean, seed and stem the chile pods, then rehydrate by steeping in boiling water for about 15 minutes. Combine pods with garlic in the blender. Add cumin and oregano. Add to caribe mixture.
