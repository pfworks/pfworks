                     Texas style mesquite bbq brisket

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1               each  brisket
  1                cup  Texas BBQ rub or every 5 lb of brisket
  2               cups  Western BBQ sauce for every 5 lb of brisket

Rub the brisket thoroughly with the rub and let stand at room temperature for 1 hour.

Build fire on one side of grill and cook meat on the other. Keep temp between 190 and 220. Place brisket on grill, fat side up and close lid. Slow cook for 8-10 hours, turning every 2-3 hours.
