                          Tomato Couscous Salad

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/4           cups  vegetable broth
  1                cup  tomato couscous
     1/2         pound  thin asparagus spears -- 1" slices
  2                     scallion -- sliced
  3        tablespoons  Italian dressing
  1                cup  cherry tomatoes -- halved
     1/4           cup  basil leaf -- finely chopped

Preheat oven to 400. Lightly spray baking sheet with olive oil spray.

In a small pot, bring broth to a boil. Stir in couscous. Remove from heat and cover. Let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.

Place asparagus and scallions on baking sheet. Toss with 1 tablespoon dressing. Roast in oven for 10-12 minutes.

In a large bowl, combine couscous, roasted vegetables cherry tomatoes, basil and remaining dressing. Toss.
