                                Tomato Pie

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  9                 in  pie shell
     1/2                yellow or red onion
  3               cups  chopped tomatoes
     1/4           cup  sliced basil
  2               cups  grated cheese (cheddar MJ or Gruyere Mozarella)
     3/4           cup  mayonnaise
  1           teaspoon  hot sauce
                        salt and pepper

Preheat oven to 350. Cook pie crust 8-10 minutes.

Sprinkle crust with onions, top with tomatoes and sprinkle basil over top.

Mix cheese, mayonnaise, hot sauce, salt and pepper and spread on top of tomatoes.

Cook until browned and bubbly, 25-45 minutes.
