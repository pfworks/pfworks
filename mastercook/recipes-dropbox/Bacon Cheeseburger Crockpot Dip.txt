                     Bacon Cheeseburger Crockpot Dip

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  ground beef
  8             ounces  cream cheese -- cubed
  2               cups  cheddar cheese -- shredded
  10            ounces  diced tomatoes with chiles
  6             ounces  cooked bacon -- chopped
  1           teaspoon  dried parsley

Brown meat.

Drain and place in large skillet.

Over low heat, stir in cheese, tomatoes with juice, and all but 2 tbspns bacon.

Cook while stirring until heated through and blended.

Pour into 2 quart crock pot.

Cover and cook on low for 2-4 hours, stirring occasionally.

Stir in parsley and remaining bacom just before serving.
