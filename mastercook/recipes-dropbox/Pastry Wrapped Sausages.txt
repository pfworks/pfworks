                         Pastry Wrapped Sausages

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        nonstick spray
  2                     8 oz tubes refrigerated crescent rolls
  16         teaspoons  honey mustard
  16       tablespoons  sharp cheddar cheese -- coarsely grated
  4                     fully cooked sausages -- quartered lengthwise

Spray heavy large baking sheet with nonstick spray. Unroll dough on work surface, separate along perforations into individual triangles. Spread each triangle with 1 tspn mustard. Sprinkle each with 1 tbspn cheese. Arrange 1 sausage quarter on triangle, parallel to short side, leaving 1/2" border. Fold short side of dough over sausage; continue to roll up. Arrange sausage roll, seam side down, on prepared sheet. Repeat.

Preheat oven to 375. Bake sausages until dough is golden, about 20 minutes.

Yield:
  "16 each"
