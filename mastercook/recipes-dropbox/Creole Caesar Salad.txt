                           Creole Caesar Salad

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  romaine lettuce -- washed and cut into bite sized pieces
                        Parmesan cheese -- grated
                        Creole Croutons
                        Creole Caesar Dressing

In large bowl, toss romaine with the dressing, using approximately 3 tablespoons of dressing per serving. Serve on chilled salad plates; top with croutons and parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.
