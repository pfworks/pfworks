                      Spicy Carrot and Pecan Souffle

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             pounds  carrots -- scraped and quartered
  8        tablespoons  butter -- cut into chunks and softened
     1/4           cup  sugar
     1/4           cup  milk
  3              large  egg
     1/2           cup  chopped pecan
  2        tablespoons  all-purpose flour
  1           teaspoon  baking powder
     1/4      teaspoon  ground cinnamon
     1/8      teaspoon  ground nutmeg

Preheat oven to 350.

Place carrots in large saucepan with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to moderate, cook till carrots are tender, about 20 minutes, and drain.

Place carrots in fiid processor, and butter and sugar, and process till well blended. Add all remaining ingredients and process until smooth, scraping down sides. Scrape into 1.5 to 2 quart souffle dish and bake till set, about 45 minutes.
