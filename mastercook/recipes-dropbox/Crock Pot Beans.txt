                             Crock Pot Beans

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  dried pinto beans
  2               cups  yellow onion -- minced
     1/4           cup  pure chile powder
  3        tablespoons  salt
  1                cup  bacon ends -- chopped

Wash beans and sort thru, removing foreign particles and broken beans. Cover by cold water in crock pot by 6 inches and soak overnight.


Be sure beans remained covered with water overnight.

Add onions, chile powder, salt, bacon and stir to blend. Set crockpot to high until simmering then switch to low. Check from time to time, stirring and adding water as needed.
