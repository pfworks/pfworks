                          Fresh Strawberry Cake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        cake
  1                box  vanilla, white or yellow cake mix
  3             ounces  strawberry gelatin powder
  4                     egg
  1                cup  vegetable oil
     1/4           cup  water
  1                cup  fresh strawberries (1 pint)
                        Icing
  4             ounces  cream cheese -- softened
     1/3           cup  butter -- softened
  2               cups  powdered sugar
  1           teaspoon  vanilla extract
  1         tablespoon  milk

Preheat oven to 350.

Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray.

Rinse and dry strawberries. Remove stems and dice. Crush in ziploc bag.

Combine cake mix with gelatin powder. Stir in oil, eggs and water. Add in 1 cup of strawberries.

Pour batter into dish and bake for 30-33 minutes. Cool completely

Mix butter and cream cheese until creamy and smooth. Mix in powdered sugar, vanilla extract and milk. Mix until smooth and creamy. 

Frost cake.
