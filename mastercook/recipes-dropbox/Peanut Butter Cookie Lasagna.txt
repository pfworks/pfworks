                       Peanut Butter Cookie Lasagna

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                box  nutter butter cookies
  1                cup  cream
                        sugar
                        peanut butter cups
     1/2           cup  peanut butter -- melted
  1                box  instant or regular vanilla pudding -- prepared

Whip cream with a little sugar.

Line 8x8 pan with cookies.

Drizzle with peanut butter.

Add 1/2 of the pudding.

Add 1/2 of the cream.

Top with chopped peanut butter cups.

Repeat.

Decorate top with crushed nutter butters, chopped peanut butter cups and peanut butter drizzle.

Refrigerate overnight.
