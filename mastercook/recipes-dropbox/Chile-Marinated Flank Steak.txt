                       Chile-Marinated Flank Steak

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2             cloves  garlic -- coarsely chopped
  1                cup  extra virgin olive oil
     1/2           cup  cilantro leaves -- rinsed and patted dry
     1/2           cup  ancho chile paste
     1/2           cup  unsweetened cocoa
  2                     juice of lime
                        salt and pepper
  2             pounds  flank steak
                        lime wedge

Mash the garlic into a paste.

Pour olive oiil into blender, add cilantro and blend on high. Add the garlic, ancho paste, cocoa and lime juice and blend to combine. Season with salt an pepper.

Trim fat from steak and pat dry. Place in a baking dish and season lightly on both sides with salt and pepper. Cover both sides of meat with marinade. Cover and chill for 24 hours.

Bring flank steak to room temp.

Sear meat on both sides. Move meat to cooler part of grill and grill, turning once until medium rare. Transfer to platter and let rest 15 minutes.

Thinly slice meat against grain and serve with lime wedges.
