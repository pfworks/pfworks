                                Cloud Eggs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  6                     eggs
                        cheese, bacon, ham or veggies

Prehat oven to 400. Prepare a parchment lined baking sheet.

Separate eggs (keep yolks separate) and beat whites until stiff peaks form. Fold  in cheese, veggies and/or meat.

Scoop 1/2 cup egg whites onto parchment into a circle. Create well in center. Bake for 3 minutes. Drop yolk in center and bake for 4 minutes. Serve.
