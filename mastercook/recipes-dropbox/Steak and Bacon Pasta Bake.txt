                        Steak and Bacon Pasta Bake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  grilled steak or roast beef -- bite size pieces
  6             slices  crisp cooked bacon -- chopped
  6               cups  cooked penne or rigatoni
  3               cups  Spicy Tomato Compote
  3               cups  grated cheddar or mozzarella
                        Spicy Tomato Compote
  4             cloves  garlic -- minced
  6        tablespoons  olive oil
  10             large  ripe tomato -- diced
  2        tablespoons  brown sugar
  6        tablespoons  balsamic vinegar
     1/2      teaspoon  red pepper flakes
                        salt and pepper

Saute garlic in the oil over medium heat for a minute. Add tomatoes, brown sugar, chile flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook until it takes on a jam-like consistency. Add balsamic vinegar in the final minute.

Oil a 9x9 baking dish. Toss pasta, beef, bacon and tomato compote. Place half in dish, top with half of cheese; repeat. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
