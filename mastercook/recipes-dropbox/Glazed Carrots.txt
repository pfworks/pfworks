                              Glazed Carrots

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1           teaspoon  sugar -- raw
  1           teaspoon  kosher salt
  2        tablespoons  unsalted butter
  2        tablespoons  water
  6                     carrots -- peeled, cut in half lengthwise then cut in half again

Put all the ingredients in a heavy pot and cover. Cook, over very low heat, unti lthe carrots are tender. Shake pot frequently so carrots don't stick and burn. They should be done in less then 10 minutes. Don't let the carrots blacken.
