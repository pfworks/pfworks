                           Bavrarian Pot Roast

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  3             pounds  boneless chuck roast -- trimmed
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil
  1 1/4           cups  water
     3/4           cup  beer
  8             ounces  tomato sauce
     1/2           cup  onion -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  sugar
  1         tablespoon  white vinegar
  2          teaspoons  salt
  1           teaspoon  cinnamon -- ground
  1                     bay leaf
     1/2      teaspoon  ginger -- ground
     1/2      teaspoon  pepper
     1/4           cup  cornstarch
     1/4           cup  water

Brown roast on all sides in hot oil in a Dutch oven. Add 1.25 cups water and next 10 ingredients. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 2.5 to 3 hours or until roast is tender. Remove roast to a serving platter. Discard bay leaf.

Combine cornstarch and 1/4 cup water. Stir cornstarch mixture slowly into pan juices. Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until thickened.
