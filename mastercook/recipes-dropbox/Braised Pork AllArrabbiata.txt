                        Braised Pork AllArrabbiata

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2         pounds  boneless pork shoulder -- trimmed
                        kosher salt and black pepper
  2        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  10            cloves  garlic -- peeled and smashed
  1 1/2      teaspoons  red pepper flakes
  3                     14 oz cans fire roasted tomatoes
  1                cup  red wine
  5             sprigs  basil

Heat oven to 350. Season the pork all over with 2 teaspoons salt and 1 teaspoon pepper.

In a large Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over med-high. Add the pork shoulder and sear until browned on alll sides, 8-10 minutes.

Reduce heat to medium low. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes to the oil and stir to combine. Add the tomatoes, red wine and basil. Stir to combine, season with salt and lots of black pepper, then bring to a boil over med-high heat.

Cover, then transfer to oven until pork falls apart, 2.5 - 3 hours.

Shred pork with two forks in stir into sauce.
