                           Dreamy Chicken Salad

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  boneless/skinless chicken breast (or 3 lbs cooked chicken chicken chunks)
  1           teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
     1/2           cup  chopped pecans or walnuts
  2        tablespoons  crystallized ginger -- minced
                        dressing
     1/3           cup  mayonnaise
     1/3           cup  buttermilk
  2          teaspoons  Tuscan Sunset
     1/2      teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
     1/4      teaspoon  salt

If using raw chicken, simmer in water with pepper for 20 minutes until cooked, or season with pepper and bake at 325 for 20 minutes. Let cool and then chop. Place in bowl with ginger and nuts. In a separate bowl, combine dressing, then pour over chicken and combine.
