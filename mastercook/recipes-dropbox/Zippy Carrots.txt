                              Zippy Carrots

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  baby carrot
  2        tablespoons  butter
     1/4           cup  brown sugar
  2        tablespoons  dry mustard
     1/4      teaspoon  salt

Steam carrots until crisp, yet tender, and place in a medium saucepan.

Combine the remaining ingredients in a small bowl and add to carrots.

Cook over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.

Serve warm.
