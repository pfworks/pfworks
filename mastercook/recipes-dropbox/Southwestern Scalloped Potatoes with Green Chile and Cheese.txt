       Southwestern Scalloped Potatoes with Green Chile and Cheese

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/2         pounds  new potato -- scrubbed and thinly sliced
  1                cup  heavy cream
  1                cup  green chiles -- roasted and chopped
  1                cup  monterey jack or cheddar cheese -- grated
     1/2           cup  red onion -- diced
  4        tablespoons  butter
  2          teaspoons  salt
  1           teaspoon  canela -- freshly ground

Preheat oven to 350.

Lightly oil a 9x12 baking dish.

Place a thin layer of potatoes in baking dish and top with 1/4 cup cream. Top with chile, cheese, onion, butter, and sprinklings of salt and canela. Repeat layers. Bake 40-50 minutes, until the potatoes are soft and the top is golden.
