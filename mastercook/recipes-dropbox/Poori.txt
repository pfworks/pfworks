                                  Poori

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4             ounces  sieved wheatmeal flour
  4             ounces  flour
  1           teaspoon  salt
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil plus more for frying
  3 1/2         ounces  water

Put the flours and salt in a bowl. Dribble oil over top. Rub oil in with fingers so mixture resembles coarse breadcrumbs. Slowly add water to form a ball of dough. Knead on a clean work surface for 10-12 minutes or until smooth. Form a ball and rub it with 1/4 tspn of oil and seal in a bag. Set aside for 30 minutes.

Knead again and divide into 12 balls. Keep them covered. Flatten into a 5" round. Repeat and keep covered.

Put about 1" of oil in pan. Let it get very hot. Line a plate with paper towels. Put a poori in the oil. Using back of slotted spoon, push the poori into the oil with tiny, swift strokes. When puffy, turn over and cook for about 10 seconds.

Yield:
  "12 "
