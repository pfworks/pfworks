                        Drunk and Dirty Tenderloin

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1                cup  soy sauce
     1/2           cup  bourbon
     1/4           cup  worcestershire sauce
  2        tablespoons  brown sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  ground ginger
  4             cloves  garlic -- minced
  2             pounds  beef tenderloin
  2        tablespoons  coarse black pepper
  1           teaspoon  white pepper
     1/4           cup  canola or corn oil
     1/4           cup  water

Combine soy sauce, bourbon, worcestshire sauce, brown sugar, ginger and garlic in a bowl and whisk to combine. Place tenderloin in resealable bag and pour marinade over and refrigerate for 4-12 hours. Turn meat occasionally.

Prepare smoker to 200-220. Remove tenderloin and drain marinade, reserving it. Coat the tenderloin with black pepper, then white pepper. Let sit for 30 minutes.

Pour half of marinade in a saucepan and refrigerate until tenderloin is cooked. Pout the other half into a saucepan and pour in oil and water. Bring to a boil over high heat for several minutes. Keep warm over low heat.

In a heavy skillet over high heat, sear meat on all sides. Transfer to smoker and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, basting every 20 minutes until 140. Bring reserved marinade to a boil and reduce by 1/4. Slice beef and serve with sauce.
