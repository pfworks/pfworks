                  Slow Cooker Creole Chicken and Sausage

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1              pound  boneless skinless chicken breast
  12            ounces  andouille sausage -- cut into rounds
  1                cup  chopped onion
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  1 1/4           cups  chicken broth
  14 1/2        ounces  diced tomato
  3        tablespoons  tomato paste
     1/2           cup  tomato sauce
  2          teaspoons  creole seasoning
     1/4      teaspoon  cayenne
                        optional
  1         tablespoon  brown sugar
  14            ounces  black beans -- rinsed and drained
  2                     red bell pepper -- chopped
     1/2           cup  green onion -- sliced
  2        tablespoons  almond butter
                        salt
                        brown rice

Place all non-optional ingredients in slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3 hours.

Shred chicken in crockpot. Add optional ingredients, and cover and cook for 20-30 minutes. Serve over rice and top with green onions.
