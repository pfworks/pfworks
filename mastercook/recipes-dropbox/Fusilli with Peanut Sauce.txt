                        Fusilli with Peanut Sauce

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  5        tablespoons  peanut butter
  2          teaspoons  brown sugar -- if needed
  2             cloves  garlic -- chopped
  2          teaspoons  ginger root -- chopped
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil
  2        tablespoons  soy sauce
  1         tablespoon  cider vinegar
  5        tablespoons  boiling water -- from fusilli
  1         tablespoon  sesame oil
                        sesame seeds
     1/2         pound  fusilli

Bring large pot salted water to boil. Cook 10 minutes.

Combine all gredients in bowl except water. Whisk liquid gredients making a paste. Whisk in hot water by tbsp till sauce is creamy.

Drain fusilli. Add noodles to sauce. Sprinkle with sesame oil and toss. Sprinle seeds over top.

Serve within 4 hours. Don't refrig.
