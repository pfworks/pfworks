                      Grilled Peppered Dry Spareribs

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  white cane sugar
  2        tablespoons  lemon pepper
  1         tablespoon  onion salt
  2          teaspoons  celery salt
  1           teaspoon  seasoned salt
  1         tablespoon  paprika
  2          teaspoons  cajun seasoning
  1           teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
  1           teaspoon  ground coriander
     1/2      teaspoon  citric acid
                        ribs
  2                     slabs spareribs
                        apple juice
                        bbq sauce

Heat a cooker to medium to medium high.

Combine all rub ingredients and blend. Rub all over the ribs.

Oil grate and place the ribs on it bone side down and direct heat. Cover and cook for 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 hours, or until pull apart tender, turning and spraying with apple juice every 10 to 15 minutes.

Transfer ribs to a cutting board and let them rest, covered loosely with foil, for 10 to 15 minutes. Cut into individual ribs and serve with bbq sauce.
