                        Pan-Seared Steak Pizzaiola

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2 1/2    tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  1 1/2      teaspoons  oregano
     1/4      teaspoon  fennel seed -- crushed
     1/4      teaspoon  dried red pepper -- crushed
  2             cloves  garlic -- pressed
  16            ounces  can diced tomatoes in juice
  2                     16 oz boneless rib-eye steak -- 3/4" thick

Heat 2 tbspns oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add 1tspn oregano and next 3 ingredients; stir 30 seconds. Add tomatoes with juices and bring to simmer, crushing with back of fork. Reduce heat and simmer until thickened, stirring occasionaly, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Meanwhile, sprinkle steaks on both sides with salt and pepper, then crush 1/2 tspn oregano finely and sprinkle over steaks. Heat 1/2 tbspn oil in heavy med skillet over med-high heat. Add steaks. Cook about 6 minutes per side for medium rare.

Cut steaks into pieces and divide among plates. Add sauce to skillet. Bring to simmer, scraping up brown bits. Spoon sauce over steaks.
