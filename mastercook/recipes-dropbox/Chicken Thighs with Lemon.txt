                        Chicken Thighs with Lemon

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  8                     bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
                        salt
                        pepper
                        rind of half of preserved lemon -- finely chopped
                        lemon wedge

Put the olive oil into a large, heavy skillet over medium heat. Season thighs with salt and pepper and add them to skillet skin side down. Cook them like this, without moving, until fat has rendered out and deep golden brown, 15 to 30 minutes. Turn thighs over and stir rind into the fat. Continue to cook about 15 minutes more. Serve with drippings and wedges.
