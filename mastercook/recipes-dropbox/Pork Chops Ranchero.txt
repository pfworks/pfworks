                           Pork Chops Ranchero

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  6                     green new mexico chiles -- roasted, peeled, stemmed, seeded and chopped
     1/4           cup  lime juice
  2        tablespoons  vegetable oil
     1/4           cup  onion -- chopped
  2             cloves  garlic -- minced
  2          teaspoons  ground cumin
  1           teaspoon  dried oregano
     1/2      teaspoon  ground coriander
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
  4                     thick cut pork chops

Combine all ingredients except pork in a bowl and mix well. Marinate pork for 4 hours to overnight.

Remove chops and grill.

Boil remaining marinade and serve with chops.
