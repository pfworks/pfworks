                           Big Game Pizza Bowl

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 15    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  24            ounces  shredded mozzarella cheese
  1              pound  cream cheese -- softened
  7        tablespoons  extra virgin olive oil
  2          teaspoons  crushed red pepper
  2          teaspoons  kosher salt
  1           teaspoon  dried basil
  1           teaspoon  garlic powder
  1           teaspoon  dried oregano
  6             ounces  tomato sauce
  4             ounces  pepperoni -- sliced thin

Pulse the heck out of everything except the tomato sauce and pepperoni, scraping the bowl to ensure even mixing. Shape mass into big ball with wells on top. Pour in tomato sauce into wells and shingle with pepperoni.
