                                Sweet Tea

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  4                     qt sized tea bags
  2               cups  sugar

Bring 2 quarts cold water to a boil in a pot over high heat, add tea bags. Remove pot from heat and allow to steep for 4 minutes.

Meanwhile combine 2 quarts cold water with sugar in a 1 gallon jug. Remove tea bags from pot, pour hot tea into jug and stir well.

Yield:
  "1 gallon"
