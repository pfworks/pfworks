                           Sopapilla Cheesecake

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  16            ounces  cream cheese -- softened
  1                cup  white sugar
  1           teaspoon  mexican vanilla extract
  16            ounces  refrigerated crescent rolls
     3/4           cup  white sugar
  1           teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2           cup  butter -- room temp
     1/4           cup  honey

Preheat oven to 350. Prepare a 9x13 baking dish with butter or spray.

Beat cream cheese with 1 cup of sugar and vanilla until smooth.

Unroll dough, and roll each into a 9x13 rectangle. Press one into bottom of pan. Evenly spread cream cheese mixture, then cover with other dough. Stir together 3/4 cup sugar, cinnamon and butter. Dot mixture on top.

Bake until golden brown, about 30 minutes. Remove from oven and drizzle with honey. Cool completely.

Yield:
  "12 "
